01The Company

A purpose-built edge AI platform for industries that can't ship data to the cloud.

THOX.ai builds Nova, an edge inference device, and ThoxOS, the on-device operating system that runs it. The platform extends through four reinforcing software layers — magnetic clustering, a private mesh fabric, on-device quantum emulation, and an automated cloud-to-edge migration platform.

Target customers are healthcare systems, law firms, financial services, and government agencies. Every industry where data sovereignty is non-negotiable, and where cloud AI is structurally incompatible with the workflow.

02The Platform

Five reinforcing layers. Each is a revenue line. Each strengthens the others.

L1 · HARDWARE

THOX Nova™

Edge inference device

The on-prem compute platform. Runs 7B–32B models locally with predictable cost, low latency, and full data sovereignty.

L2 · INTERCONNECT

MagStack™

Magnetic clustering

Snap 1, 2, 4, or 8 devices face-to-back. Scale to 200B-parameter models and 32-qubit quantum emulation per cluster.

L3 · MESH

MeshStack™

Cross-platform fabric

iOS, Android, macOS, Windows. Turns personal devices into a private mesh. Recurring SaaS layered on every Nova.

L4 · QUANTUM

ThoxQuantum™

On-device emulation

GPU-accelerated quantum simulation native to ThoxOS. Up to 32 qubits on an 8× MagStack. HIPAA-compliant. Air-gapped.

L5 · MIGRATION

ThoxMigrate™

Cloud-to-edge platform

One API_BASE_URL change moves a customer off OpenAI or Anthropic onto THOX. Three AI teams. Gated access. Platform revenue.
