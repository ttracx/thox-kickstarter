## 8. Product positioning

ThoxKey:

- Entry hardware tier and portable private AI USB drive.
- Public level: launches a local private AI workspace from a host computer.
- Do not frame as a security key unless separately verified.

ThoxMini:

- USB-C private compute stick running ThoxOS Mini.
- Public level: compact local-first compute node for builders and labs.
- Keep boot time, model size, and performance claims proof-gated.

ThoxMini Air:

- Wireless pocket or keychain AI node with MeshStack onboarding.
- Public level: wireless local-first node that pairs through MeshStack.
- Keep battery, charging, secure element, IP rating, thermal, and performance claims proof-gated unless approved.

ThoxClip:

- Phone-attached private AI companion.
- Public level: input, status, and local workflow bridge for MeshStack.
- Do not claim platform certification, official accessory status, secure triggers, or charge-through behavior without proof.

MeshStack:

- Companion app and private device mesh.
- Use five scenes: Identity, Pair, Connect, Monitor, Chat.
- Approved privacy phrases only: `data stays on your devices`, `works offline on LAN after pairing`, `uses the WireGuard protocol`, `coordinator never sees plaintext`.

## 9. Compliance guardrails

These rules are a hard gate on public assets. Severity error blocks final status.

Forbidden in public copy:

- Part, chip, board, module, supplier, or vendor names unless Tommy explicitly approves.
- TOPS figures and hard performance numbers unless verified and approved.
- Internal algorithm names.
- Patent Pending or patent-status claims. Use `proprietary` only if needed.
- Absolute or regulatory claims: military grade, unhackable, no central server, we cannot decrypt, HIPAA/GDPR/ITAR compliant.
- Unheld certification claims, official accessory claims, or bare certification marks.
- Supplier, contract manufacturer, inactive partner, city, or state names.
- Retired product names: ThoxAir, ThoxOnStick, ThoxStick, ThoxNova as a reward, Pro/Lite/Max/Ultra variants.
- Retired colorway names. Allowed colorways are Matte Black, Space Gray, Arctic White. Stretch-only finishes are Sage, Midnight, Copper, Sand after $400K unlock context.
- Sadie Weaving or Victoria Weyland in founder, legal, investor, or Kickstarter-page contexts. Public founding team is Tommy Xaypanya + Craig Ross only. Legal/patent docs may use Phamy Xaypanya only when required.

Warnings to report but not block:

- Hard latency figures.
- Context-window figures.
- Language-count claims.
- Named third-party model lists.
- Double-hyphen-as-dash.
- Off-token colors or fonts.
- Any social/video prompt that asks for a visual style outside the THOX.ai design system.

Every final asset gets a compliance report entry with filename, scan date, pass/fail, design-system pass/fail, and violations.
