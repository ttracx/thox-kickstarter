# THOX Kickstarter Asset QA Checklist

## Date and scope

- [ ] Launch date is July 9, 2026 everywhere.
- [ ] Close date is August 8, 2026 everywhere it appears.
- [ ] Product scope is ThoxKey, ThoxMini, ThoxMini Air, ThoxClip, MeshStack.
- [ ] No ThoxAir, ThoxOnStick, ThoxStick, or Nova-as-reward in public copy.
- [ ] No city or state in public output.

## Design system

- [ ] Display text uses Xolonium.
- [ ] Body text uses Inter.
- [ ] Labels, SKUs, telemetry, and code use JetBrains Mono.
- [ ] Background is `#09090B` or `#000000`.
- [ ] Cards use `#18181B` or approved MeshStack card surfaces.
- [ ] CTA and active states use `#10B981`.
- [ ] Hover or highlights use `#34D399`.
- [ ] Purple appears only in MagStack context.
- [ ] MeshStack does not use purple.
- [ ] No off-brand fonts, colors, or legacy prototype shell styles are used in new campaign assets.
- [ ] Light DOCX/print exports use white background, black headings, gray body, emerald accents.

## Claims

- [ ] Every public claim is feature-level and proof-safe.
- [ ] Part, chip, vendor, supplier, and contract-manufacturer names are excluded.
- [ ] No TOPS figures or hard performance numbers unless approved.
- [ ] No patent-status claim.
- [ ] No absolute security claim.
- [ ] No regulatory compliance claim without counsel sign-off.
- [ ] No unheld certification claim.

## Kickstarter readiness

- [ ] Working prototype proof is shown.
- [ ] Renders are labeled or clearly used as diagrams.
- [ ] AI-generated content is disclosed if used.
- [ ] Project image is 16:9, with 1024 x 576 export plus large source master.
- [ ] Story images are at least 700 px wide and under 50 MB.
- [ ] Reward images are 3:2, at least 348 x 232, and under 50 MB.
- [ ] Video export is MP4 H.264 and under 5 GB.
- [ ] FAQ and Risks are complete.
