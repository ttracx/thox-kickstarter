# Runbook 05: Post-funding (T+31 onward)

After the campaign closes. Goal: backer trust through fulfillment +
BackerKit add-on revenue + retention for the post-shipment community.
Cadence: 3 posts/week per platform, dropping to 1-2/week after T+90.

## T+31 to T+33: rest

The team takes 48-72 hours off. Reply backlog can wait 48 hours; this
is the only window in the entire 6-month timeline where it can.

Crisis-lead remains on call.

## T+34 (Mon Sep 14 2026): re-engagement post

The first post-funding post. Sets the tone for the fulfillment phase.

- [ ] Theme: "We're back, here's the plan"
- [ ] Hero: founder talking head (real photo) on dark navy backdrop
      with the funded amount as text overlay
- [ ] Caption: 3 paragraphs - thank you, what we're building this
      month, when the next update lands (bi-weekly build log).
- [ ] Email blast same day: "Update 01 - fulfillment kickoff" via
      `../templates/weekly-update.md`.

## Bi-weekly build log cadence

Every other Friday at 11am PT, on all 7 platforms. Pattern:

| Day | Theme | Visual | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Friday | "Build log 0N from manufacturing" | Real photo of the latest milestone | Craig (hardware spokesperson) |

Anchor the build log to a real manufacturing milestone:

- Build log 02 (T+44): first PCB lot arrives (see worked example
  `../social/posts/06-post-funding/2026-09-25-pcb-arrival-build-log.md`)
- Build log 03 (T+58): enclosure tooling complete + first-article
  enclosures off the line
- Build log 04 (T+72): BackerKit address confirmation open + first 100
  units assembled
- Build log 05 (T+86): first 100 ship
- Build log 06 (T+100): international shipping begins
- Build log 07 (T+114): MagStack Air 4-pack and 8-pack assembly
- Build log 08 (T+128): final batch ships; we are fulfilling

If a milestone slips (and it will), publish a "we slipped, here's why"
update. Backers reward transparency; they punish silence.

## BackerKit pivot (T+45 to T+60)

BackerKit is where post-funding revenue comes from. The pivot:

- [ ] T+45: BackerKit add-on store opens. Backers confirm addresses
      and add accessory tiers (keychain, coin, MagStack Air upgrades,
      shipping protection).
- [ ] Email blast: "BackerKit is live" via
      `../templates/weekly-update.md`.
- [ ] Social cadence increase: 4 posts/week for 2 weeks while the
      BackerKit conversion window is open.
- [ ] BackerKit add-on revenue typically delivers 30-50% of campaign
      total in additional pledges. Plan for this revenue in the
      forecast.

## Press hit recap

- [ ] T+45: send the "post-funding press kit" with the final funded
      amount + the fulfillment schedule to the press list. Many
      outlets do a "funded" recap article that drives BackerKit
      conversions.
- [ ] Pin the best press hit to all 7 platform profiles.

## Community migration

The Kickstarter comments section dies after T+30. Migrate the active
community to:

- [ ] Discord server (open at T+34; invite-only at first to keep
      signal high)
- [ ] Backer-only newsletter (segment the email list)
- [ ] LinkedIn page (for the B2B / engineering crowd)

Goal: by T+90, the Discord has 1,000+ active members and the
newsletter has a 40%+ open rate.

## What stops being a priority

- TikTok: keep posting but drop cadence. The TikTok audience does not
  follow fulfillment posts well; it's a discovery platform, not a
  retention one.
- Reddit: stop posting in r/Kickstarter (the audience there only
  engages during active campaigns). Continue in r/raspberry_pi and
  r/LocalLLaMA when there's a technical milestone worth sharing.
- Threads: continue cross-posting from IG but do not generate
  Threads-specific content.

## What becomes a priority

- LinkedIn: the engineering / dev audience. Build-log posts perform
  best here long-term.
- YouTube: long-form build log videos (3-5 min, not Shorts). YT search
  traffic for "local AI device review" picks up these videos.
- Email newsletter: the highest-conversion channel for BackerKit
  add-ons and post-fulfillment community retention.

## Post-funding acceptance gate (T+90)

- [ ] BackerKit add-on revenue ≥ 30% of campaign total
- [ ] BackerKit address confirmation ≥ 90% of backers
- [ ] Discord ≥ 1,000 members
- [ ] Newsletter open rate ≥ 40%
- [ ] All bi-weekly build logs published on schedule
- [ ] Reply SLA ≤ 6 hours on all platforms

## When fulfillment ships (T+150 to T+200)

The "It's in your hands" celebration. Plan a multi-platform takeover
where backers post unboxing UGC. Provide:

- [ ] Branded unboxing hashtag (e.g., `#THOXunboxed`)
- [ ] Repost queue for the first 100 unboxing posts (manual selection;
      automated reposters look spammy)
- [ ] BackerKit followup email with the unboxing hashtag and a
      reminder that they have a 1-year warranty + support email

## Handoff

After T+200, transition from "campaign operations" to "product
operations". The communications cadence becomes whatever the product
team owns going forward. Archive the runbooks; revive when the next
Kickstarter approaches.
