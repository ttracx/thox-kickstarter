# MagStack Cluster Dock unboxing - 45s social cut

## Posting plan

- Channel: Instagram Reels + TikTok + LinkedIn + X
- Date: T-14 days (July 29, 2026)
- Founder voice: Craig (hardware) leads and closes; Tommy delivers the routing line
- Render dependencies: 4-node MagStack hero + cluster dock side callout
- Position in cadence: this is the hero stack reveal of the campaign

## Script (45s)

### Beat 1 (0 to 5s): Hook

On camera: a flat shipping box, larger and shallower than the other
SKUs, on a dark walnut desk. Brand-cyan accent light from camera-right.

Voiceover (Craig): "This is the MagStack Cluster Dock. The hero stack."

### Beat 2 (5 to 15s): Open the box

On camera: hands lift the lid. The cluster dock is laid flat in the
tray, base plate up. Hands flip it over. Macro on the magnetic pad on
top and the USB-C hub on the side. Cable management channel visible.

Voiceover (Craig): "Heavy base plate. Magnetic pad on top. USB-C hub
on the side. Cable channel routes power and data through the spine."

### Beat 3 (15 to 30s): The stack assembly

On camera: hands place one ThoxMini Air onto the magnetic pad. Then a
second clicks onto the halo ring of the first. Then a third. Then a
fourth. Each unit snaps with a real Foley snap. All four LED bars
light cyan in sequence.

Voiceover (Craig): "One unit. Two. Three. Four. Magnets do the work.
No screws. No cables between units."

### Beat 4 (30 to 40s): Routing reveal

On camera: cut to a laptop screen showing the THOXCore router status:
four nodes online, traffic flowing through the dock.

Voiceover (Tommy): "Four nodes routing through THOXCore. The dock is
the spine. The router decides where each request lands."

### Beat 5 (40 to 45s): CTA

On camera: cut to the THOX logo overlay over a clean wide shot of the
four-node stack on the dock, all LED bars lit. Kickstarter URL under
the logo.

Voiceover (Craig): "Back us on Kickstarter. August twelve."

## Compliance

- No medical claims.
- No financial claims.
- No competitor names visible or spoken.
- The four-node configuration matches the MagStack Air documented
  maximum. Do not stage a five-or-more-node configuration on camera.
- THOXCore router status screen must show real routing, not a mock.
  Capture from a live MagStack Air session before the shoot.

  primary SKU price. The CTA in this cut does not call a price out
  loud (intentional). The price appears in the rewards page only.
