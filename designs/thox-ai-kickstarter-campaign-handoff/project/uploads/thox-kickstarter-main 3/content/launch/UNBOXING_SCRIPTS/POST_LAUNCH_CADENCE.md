## Phase 5: Campaign window (T+0 to T+30, August 12 to September 11)

Live campaign. Posting cadence is event-driven (manufacturing peeks +
backer milestones) rather than calendar-driven. The unboxing scripts
in this directory are referenced during campaign updates whenever a
device-specific question comes up in the comments.

| Trigger | Asset | Channel | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Funded | Campaign update: "Funded." + thanks card | All channels | Same day. |
| First stretch goal hit | Repost the relevant unboxing cut | All channels | Tie the stretch goal to the SKU. |
| Manufacturing milestone (first PCB run) | Behind-the-scenes from the bench | LinkedIn + IG | 30 s. |
| Backer count milestones (100, 500, 1k) | Thanks card + founder voice note | IG Stories + X | Voice only, 20 s. |
| Comment surge on a specific SKU | Repost that SKU's unboxing cut | The channel where the surge happened | Reuse the existing cut. |
| Final 72 hours | Countdown cards "72 hours", "48", "24" | All channels | Static. |
| Final 24 hours | Reshare the 90-second hero | All channels | Pinned. |
| Campaign close | Thanks card + recap reel | All channels | 60 s recap reel cuts together highlights from every unboxing in this directory. |

## Channel notes

- **Instagram Reels:** vertical 9:16, 1080x1920. Captions burned in.
- **TikTok:** vertical 9:16, 1080x1920. Captions burned in.
- **LinkedIn:** square 1:1 or 4:5, 1080x1080 or 1080x1350. Captions
  burned in. Longer text caption.
- **X:** square 1:1, 1080x1080. Captions burned in. Short text caption.
- **YouTube Shorts:** vertical 9:16, 1080x1920. Captions burned in.
  Longer text caption with chapter markers.
