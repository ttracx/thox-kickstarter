# ThoxMini unboxing - 45s social cut

## Posting plan

- Channel: Instagram Reels + TikTok + LinkedIn + X

- Founder voice: Craig leads; Tommy delivers the closing CTA
- Render dependencies: ThoxMini iso hero render + exploded view

## Script (45s)

### Beat 1 (0 to 5s): Hook

On camera: a sealed THOX shipping box on a dark walnut desk. Brand-cyan
accent light from camera-right. Hands enter from frame-bottom.

Voiceover (Craig): "This is ThoxMini. Desktop edge AI in your hand."

### Beat 2 (5 to 15s): Open the box

On camera: hands lift the lid. Foam tray inside. Reveal the assembled
ThoxMini in matte black. Macro on the THOX deboss on the front face,
then on the USB-C port on the rear.

Voiceover (Craig): "Single-node enclosure. THOX deboss on the front.
USB-C on the back. Built around the Luckfox Pico Mini B."

### Beat 3 (15 to 28s): Plug and first boot

On camera: USB-C cable connects to a MacBook. The LED bar on the
ThoxMini lights cyan. Cut to the laptop screen: a status badge fades
in that reads "thoxmini-luckfox online".

Voiceover (Tommy): "Plug it in. Eight seconds to boot. Edge skills are
pre-loaded. Local-first. No cloud round-trip."

### Beat 4 (28 to 38s): Skill in action

On camera: a single skill runs on the laptop screen against the
ThoxMini gateway at `127.0.0.1:18790`. The latency overlay shows the
on-device response.

Voiceover (Tommy): "Routed through THOXCore. Your data stays on the
device unless you say otherwise."

### Beat 5 (38 to 45s): CTA

On camera: cut to the THOX logo overlay over a clean shot of the
ThoxMini with the LED bar lit. Kickstarter URL under the logo.

Voiceover (Tommy): "Sixty-nine dollars on Kickstarter. August twelve.
Back us."
