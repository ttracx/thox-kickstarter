# ThoxNova unboxing - 60s social cut

## Posting plan

- Channel: Instagram Reels + TikTok + LinkedIn + YouTube Shorts
- Date: T-17 days (July 26, 2026)
- Founder voice: Tommy leads the software story; Craig closes on hardware and CTA
- Render dependencies: ThoxNova iso hero + exploded + LattePanda N100 callout
- Position in cadence: this is the flagship unboxing of the campaign

## Script (60s)

### Beat 1 (0 to 6s): Hook

On camera: a larger THOX shipping box on a dark walnut desk. Brand-cyan
accent light from camera-right. Two hands enter from frame-bottom.

Voiceover (Tommy): "This is ThoxNova. The flagship."

### Beat 2 (6 to 16s): Open the box

On camera: hands lift the lid. Foam tray. Reveal the ThoxNova
enclosure, matte black, larger footprint than ThoxMini. Macro on the
THOX deboss on the front face, then the rear I/O panel.

Voiceover (Craig): "Larger enclosure. Real heatsink under the lid. Two
USB-C, one USB-A, gigabit ethernet, and a barrel jack for the bench
supply."

### Beat 3 (16 to 30s): The x86 story

On camera: an exploded render fades in beside the unit on the desk,
showing the LattePanda N100 inside. Cut back to the live unit with
power plugged in. LED bar lights cyan.

Voiceover (Tommy): "Intel x86 inside. The LattePanda N100. That gives
us a wider runtime story than CUDA-only boards. SYCL on the iGPU,
Vulkan compute, CPU fallback when you need it."

### Beat 4 (30 to 45s): Plug and first boot

On camera: power connects. The LED bar lights cyan. Cut to a monitor:
ThoxNova boots into the THOX desktop shell. Skill catalog visible.

Voiceover (Tommy): "Boots into the THOX desktop shell. The skill
catalog is local. Routing through THOXCore. Your prompts and your
files stay on the box unless you push them out."

### Beat 5 (45 to 55s): Scale story

On camera: a wider shot. ThoxNova on the desk, with a ThoxMini and a
ThoxClip in the same frame for scale. Cut to a render of the
device-family lineup.

Voiceover (Craig): "Same THOX brand language. Same THOXCore router.
ThoxNova is the desktop. ThoxMini is the edge. ThoxClip is the pocket.
Pick the form factor. The software is the same."

### Beat 6 (55 to 60s): CTA

On camera: cut to the THOX logo overlay over a clean shot of the
ThoxNova. Kickstarter URL under the logo.

Voiceover (Craig): "Four hundred ninety-nine dollars. Back us on
Kickstarter. August twelve."

## Compliance

- No medical claims.
- No financial claims.
- No competitor names visible or spoken. "Intel x86" and "LattePanda
  N100" are component-name references, not competitor brand callouts,
  and are allowed.
- The SYCL, Vulkan compute, and CPU-fallback claims are accurate
  against the shipping runtime stack. If any of those three is not
  in the shipping firmware on the morning of posting, cut the
  reference.
- "Four hundred ninety-nine dollars" verbal claim is the Kickstarter
  Early-Bird base. Verify against the rewards page the morning of
  posting.
- "August twelve" verbal claim verifiable against the Kickstarter URL.
