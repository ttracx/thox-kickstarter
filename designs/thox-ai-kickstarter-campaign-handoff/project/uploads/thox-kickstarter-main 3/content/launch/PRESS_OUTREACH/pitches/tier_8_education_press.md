# tier_8_education_press.md

Education press. Targets the THOXKey EDU angle: sub-$50 AI literacy device, open syllabus inserts, community college and continuing-ed pricing.

**Lead angle for this tier**: education access. THOXKey as a hands-on AI literacy device for community colleges, continuing-ed programs, and intro AI curricula.
**Recommended template sequence**: initial_outreach -> demo_offer (with syllabus insert) -> follow_up_3_day.

## Embargo policy

Education press generally accepts embargoes when terms are documented. Pitch at T-3 (close to launch) since education buyers respond to launch news, not pre-launch teasers.

## Common dos and don'ts for Tier 8

- **Do**: pair the pitch with the syllabus insert from `ttracx/thox-key/content/outreach/SAMPLE_SYLLABUS_INSERT.md`. Education press values curricular materials.
- **Do**: mention FERPA awareness explicitly. The privacy story is the lead for this tier, but FERPA compliance is the trust signal that gets adoption.
- **Do**: offer demo units to academic reviewers (one to two units for an AI faculty member's classroom).
- **Don't**: lead with the consumer product family. Tier 8 cares about the EDU SKU and the syllabus, not the prosumer device family.
- **Don't**: claim FERPA compliance unless `docs/PRE_LAUNCH_COMPLIANCE.md` has the FERPA section confirmed and signed off by counsel. If the FERPA section is still open, frame as "FERPA-aware design" not "FERPA compliant."
