1. Private on-device AI (no cloud round-trip, no telemetry by default)

3. MagStack clustering (4-8 nodes magnetically chain, child-safe magnet model)
4. EDU vertical (THOXKey EDU classroom kit)

Budget envelopes use general bands (USD). Real numbers depend on Kickstarter funding tier:
- $250K floor: attend cheap local + virtual only

- $1.5M+ stretch: add CES + KubeCon NA + TechCrunch Disrupt exhibits
