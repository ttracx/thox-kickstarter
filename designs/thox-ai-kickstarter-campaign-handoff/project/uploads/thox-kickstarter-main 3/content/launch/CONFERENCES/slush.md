# Slush (Helsinki)

## Overview

- Typical dates: late Nov / early Dec 2026 (verify against slush.org)
- Location: Helsinki, Finland (Messukeskus)
- URL: https://www.slush.org
- Audience: European VCs, founders, corporate venture, EU press
- Size: ~13K attendees; highest European investor density of any single event

## Why THOX

Slush is the single most important European event for investor + press exposure. THOX has a credible EU angle: the GDPR-by-architecture, no-cloud-telemetry, data-stays-on-device story plays harder in Helsinki than it does in San Francisco. EU investors and EU regulators reward privacy-first hardware in a way US investors often discount.

Slush also opens the door to Nordic + Baltic enterprise design partners who are aggressive about on-prem AI.

## ROI angle

- Primary: investor introductions. Target 20+ qualified VC conversations + 5 corporate-venture conversations.
- Secondary: 5-10 EU press conversations (Sifted, TechCrunch EU, Wired UK, Tagesspiegel Background, Le Monde).
- Tertiary: 2-3 EU enterprise design-partner conversations (think Volvo, Nokia, ABB, Ericsson labs).

## Decision criteria

- Default decision: attend without booth. Slush booths are expensive and the value is in the side meetings, not the hall.
- Booth upgrade decision gate: Kickstarter $1M+ AND at least 2 EU enterprise conversations already in motion.
- Hard skip only if EU travel budget cannot stretch (do not skip purely for cost; Slush is high-leverage).

## Budget breakdown

- Conference registration: ~$1K-$2K early-bird; up to $4K late
- Booth (if exhibiting): $10K-$30K depending on tier
- Travel: $1.5K-$2.5K Austin-to-Helsinki + lodging $300-$500/night (Helsinki is dense in conference week, book early)
- Per-diem: $200/day Helsinki conference week
- Collateral: $500 printed, $1-2K THOXKey giveaway (50 units)
- Total envelope: $5K (attend-only, 1 person) to $40K (full booth + 2 staff)

## Booth / demo configuration

If exhibiting:
- SKUs to demo: THOXKey baseline, MagStack 4-node ring, ThoxClip
- Live demo: identical Gemma 3 prompt running on THOX device + on cloud LLM with WireShark side-by-side showing zero network egress from THOX
- Signage: "GDPR by architecture, not by audit." (EU angle headline)
- Handout: 1-page GDPR + Data Act compliance brief
- Lead capture: opt-in only, language pre-reviewed against GDPR consent standards

## Speaker submission

- Slush Stages CFP typical deadline: late Jun / early Jul 2026; verify
- THOX talk title placeholders:
  - "GDPR by architecture: building AI that cannot leak data"
  - "Why we built our hardware in the open and shipped on Kickstarter"
  - "The American startup that came to Helsinki for the regulators"
- Theme fit: Slush stages favor founder-led narratives with a sharp opinion. Pick a contrarian thesis.

## Networking strategy

- Slush side events are where the actual investor meetings happen
- Target dinners: Atomico portfolio dinner, Northzone founder dinner, Index Ventures EU dinner (request invites via warm intros)
- Helsinki sauna meetings are a Slush institution; accept any sauna invitation
- Target meet list: 10 named EU VCs + 5 named corp-dev leads from EU enterprises

## Lead capture

THOXKey serialized opt-in but with EU-tuned consent language. Critical: do not export EU lead data to non-EU systems without a compliance review.

## Post-event nurture

- T+24h: thank-you in English; offer to follow up in German/French/Finnish if mother-tongue
- T+7d: investor deck send to any qualified VC conversation
- T+14d: schedule 1:1 calls for any active VC conversation
- T+30d: EU pilot proposal for any enterprise conversation
- T+60d: confirm whether to add Web Summit Lisbon to follow-on travel for nurture
