# SXSW 2027 (Austin)

## Overview

- Typical dates: early-to-mid Mar 2027 (verify against sxsw.com)
- Location: Austin, Texas (multiple venues)
- URL: https://www.sxsw.com
- Audience: mixed creative + tech + film + music + brand; high media density
- Size: tens of thousands across tracks; AI / tech track has grown significantly

## Why THOX

SXSW is the home-turf advantage event. THOX is Austin-based; SXSW is a chance to leverage local logistics + venue access + lower travel costs + media density without committing CES-tier budget.

The mixed creative + technical audience also fits the THOX brand voice better than pure-tech events. THOXKey EDU, MagStack physical-form-factor narrative, and the "private AI you can hold" story all play to SXSW's creator-economy + maker-curious + brand-strategist attendees.

## ROI angle

- Primary: brand-press conversations. Target 10+ named creator-economy + brand-strategy press hits.
- Secondary: 5-10 partnership conversations with creator-tool companies (Descript, Notion, Figma, etc.).
- Tertiary: local Austin reinforcement; SXSW networking compounds across the year for hometown founders.

## Decision criteria

- Default decision: speak track if accepted; exhibit at SXSW Trade Show if affordable.
- Hard skip is unusual given home turf + low marginal travel cost.
- House-rental strategy: Austin home-team can host a THOX house off-campus instead of paying SXSW booth rates.

## Budget breakdown

- Conference registration: ~$1.5K-$2K SXSW Interactive badge per attendee
- SXSW Trade Show booth: $5K-$15K (cheaper than CES + KubeCon)
- Off-campus house rental + meetups: $5K-$15K (the home-team play)
- Travel: $0 (Austin-based)
- Per-diem: $100/day local
- Collateral: $1K printed, $3-5K THOXKey giveaway (100 units serialized)
- Total envelope: $5K (badges + giveaway, no booth) to $25K (booth + house + giveaway)

## Booth / demo configuration

- SKUs to demo: full SKU lineup focused on consumer-facing units
- Live demo: creator workflow + THOXKey integration (Descript-style demo)
- Signage: SXSW-tuned, more creative-economy framing; less enterprise polish
- Handout: creator-tool integration one-pager
- Lead capture: THOXKey serialized opt-in

## House strategy (alternative to booth)

If skipping the official booth:
- Rent a downtown Austin house for SXSW week
- Host 3-5 invite-only demos with curated press + partners
- Cost is comparable to a small booth, signal is much higher
- Host one open evening event for community
- Coordinate with Austin makerspace + EFF Austin + local tech-press

## Speaker submission

- SXSW PanelPicker typical deadline: early Aug 2026 (for SXSW 2027); verify
- THOX talk title placeholders:
  - "We made the Kickstarter, then we made the thing: an Austin hardware story"
  - "Private AI for creators: the case against the cloud"
  - "MagStack: when hardware design is the user interface"
- Theme fit: SXSW PanelPicker rewards creator-curious + narrative-led pitches. Avoid jargon. Lead with story.

## Networking strategy

- SXSW Austin home-team advantage means THOX can host without travel cost
- Target dinners: Capital Factory founder dinners (Austin VC), EFF Austin happy hour, local-press happy hour
- Skip large vendor parties; focus on small-group dinners
- Target meet list: 10 named creator-tool partners + 5 named Austin VCs + 5 named national press

## Lead capture

THOXKey serialized opt-in. SXSW leads can include high-celebrity opt-ins; have a tier-1 nurture flow ready for surprise high-value leads.

## Post-event nurture

- T+24h: thank-you to named press + partner leads only
- T+7d: partnership-discovery 1:1 for any active partner conversation
- T+14d: press follow-up for any active press lead
- T+30d: Austin-community newsletter publication of any noteworthy SXSW outcomes
