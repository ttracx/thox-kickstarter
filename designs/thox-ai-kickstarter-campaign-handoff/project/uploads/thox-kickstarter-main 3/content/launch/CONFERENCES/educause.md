# EDUCAUSE Annual Conference

## Overview

- Typical dates: mid-to-late Oct 2026 (verify against educause.edu)
- Location: rotates major US cities (recent: Chicago, Philadelphia, San Antonio, New Orleans)
- URL: https://www.educause.edu/annual-conference
- Audience: higher-ed CIOs, IT directors, research-computing leads, instructional-technology buyers
- Size: ~6-7K attendees; high B2B EDU buyer density

## Why THOX

EDUCAUSE is the single most important event for THOXKey EDU. Attendees are exactly the people who write district + university IT contracts. The on-device + private + no-cloud-telemetry story maps directly to FERPA + student-data-protection conversations these buyers are actively losing sleep over.

EDU IT buyers cannot adopt cloud AI tools easily because of FERPA + parental-consent + data-residency concerns. THOX sidesteps all of it by never leaving the device.

## ROI angle

- Primary: EDU B2B leads. Target 75+ qualified district / university IT leads.
- Secondary: 3-5 pilot conversations with named research-computing programs.
- Tertiary: 1-2 textbook / curriculum partner conversations (Pearson, OpenStax, etc.) for THOXKey EDU bundled content.

## Decision criteria

- Default decision: exhibit. EDU is a named THOX vertical and EDUCAUSE is the field's lighthouse event.
- Hard skip only if Kickstarter under $500K AND no Q3 EDU pilot already booked.
- Upgrade decision (premium booth + sponsorship): only if Kickstarter $2M+.

## Budget breakdown

- Conference registration: $1.5K-$2.5K per attendee; expect 2-3 staff
- Booth small footprint: $6K-$12K
- Booth furniture + AV: $2-3K
- Travel: $1K per person + lodging $250-$400/night
- Per-diem: $125/day
- Collateral: $1K printed (FERPA + child-safety one-pager critical), $3-5K THOXKey EDU classroom kit giveaway (10-20 full kits)
- Total envelope: $10K-$25K typical exhibit slot

## Booth / demo configuration

- SKUs to demo: THOXKey EDU classroom kit, MagStack Air (Pi Zero variant safer for K-12), ThoxMini Air
- Live demo: classroom scenario, 8 students each with THOXKey EDU running same prompt; teacher dashboard showing per-student progress with zero data leaving the room
- Signage: "Private AI for every student. Nothing leaves the classroom." + EDU color treatment (lighter palette, brand-approved)
- Handout: FERPA compliance one-pager + parent-consent template + curriculum-integration starter guide
- Critical: no AI-hallucination examples in demo; EDU buyers are hypersensitive; pre-curate prompts
