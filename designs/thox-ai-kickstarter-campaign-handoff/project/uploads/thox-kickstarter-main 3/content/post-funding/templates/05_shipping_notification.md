# Template 05: Shipping Notification (batch-level)

**Subject**: Your {{ reward.name }} ships this week. Tracking inside.
**Alt subject (A/B)**: {{ reward.name }} batch {{ shipment.batch_number }} is out the door.
**Preview text**: {{ shipment.carrier }} tracking number and what to expect on arrival.

**Trigger**: per-backer, fires when their unit's carrier handoff event lands in the fulfillment pipeline (the `ship` step in `app/kickstarter/rewards.py`).

**Send window**: within 4 hours of carrier handoff. Send time of day does not matter; backers are international.

**Transactional**: Yes. Ignore unsubscribe flag.

**Parametric**: per-backer. Batch number is included so backers can correlate with public batch updates.

**Variables used**:
- `{{ backer.first_name }}`
- `{{ reward.name }}`
- `{{ shipment.batch_number }}` -> e.g. "B-007"
- `{{ shipment.carrier }}` -> e.g. "FedEx", "USPS", "DHL Express"
- `{{ shipment.tracking_number }}`
- `{{ shipment.tracking_url }}` -> per-carrier deep link
- `{{ shipment.expected_delivery_date }}` -> absolute date string
- `{{ shipment.signature_required }}` -> boolean
- `{{ shipment.customs_note }}` -> populated for international shipments only

---

**Body**:

> Hi {{ backer.first_name }},
>
> Your {{ reward.name }} is in batch {{ shipment.batch_number }} and shipped today via {{ shipment.carrier }}.
>
> **Tracking number**: {{ shipment.tracking_number }}
> **Track here**: {{ shipment.tracking_url }}
> **Expected delivery**: {{ shipment.expected_delivery_date }}
> **Signature required on delivery**: {{ shipment.signature_required }}
>
> {{ shipment.customs_note }}
>
> **What is in the box**
>
> - One {{ reward.name }} unit
> - USB-C cable (1 meter, braided)
> - Quick-start card with thox.ai/quickstart QR code
> - One THOX sticker sheet
>
> If your reward tier includes add-ons or accessories, those are listed on your survey confirmation at https://thox.ai/backer/survey?b={{ backer.id }} and ship in the same box.
>
> **On arrival**
>
> 1. Open the box. Confirm the unit is intact and the serial sticker is readable.
> 2. Plug in via USB-C and follow the quickstart card.
> 3. If anything is missing, broken, or wrong, reply directly to this email within 14 days. Reply within 14 days unlocks the no-questions-asked replacement path; after 14 days we still help, but the path involves photos and a short triage.
>
> Tommy and Craig

**Footer**: standard transactional footer.

## Customs note variants (populated by region detection)

For US domestic shipments: `{{ shipment.customs_note }}` is empty.

For international (EU, UK, AU, CA, others): `{{ shipment.customs_note }}` reads:

> **International shipment note**: this package crosses a customs border. Duties, taxes, and VAT have been prepaid by us where the destination country supports prepaid delivery (US -> EU IOSS, US -> UK VAT, US -> AU GST). For destinations where prepayment is not supported, the carrier will collect duties on delivery and you will be billed at the door. The exact amount depends on your country's import rules and is outside our control. If you are surprised by a duty bill, reply and we will help you understand the line items, but we cannot waive duties the destination government has assessed.

## Notes

**When to use**: every per-unit carrier handoff. One email per shipment per backer. If a backer has multiple rewards shipping at different times (e.g. ThoxClip in batch B-001 and ThoxNova in batch B-014), they get one Template 05 per shipment.

**When NOT to use**: do not send this template until the tracking number is verified live with the carrier. A tracking number that does not resolve when the backer clicks the link is worse than waiting an extra few hours.

**Escalation criteria**: if a batch of shipping notifications goes out with bad tracking numbers (carrier ingestion lag, address validation failures, label print errors), escalate to ops lead immediately and send Template 07 with reason "shipping label issue" to the affected backers within 24 hours.

**Sequence**: Template 05 fires per-unit on ship event. Template 06 fires per-unit on delivery confirmation event (typically 3 to 10 days later for domestic, 7 to 21 days for international). Template 11 fires once per backer when their final reward in their pledge has shipped (relevant for multi-reward pledges).

**Carrier link integrity**: `{{ shipment.tracking_url }}` is generated by a per-carrier lookup table in the fulfillment service. If a new carrier is added (e.g. a regional courier for a specific country), the lookup table must be updated before any backer in that region gets a Template 05.

**Address validation gate**: Template 05 must NOT fire if the address validation step failed for this backer. The pipeline holds the unit at the `pack` step and routes the address back through ops for confirmation via a reply-to-this-email exchange.
