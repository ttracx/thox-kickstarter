# 90-day social calendar - THOX Kickstarter (Aug 2026)

The cross-platform posting cadence from T-90 (pre-launch) to T+30
(campaign close). Times are PT; convert at scheduling time for other
zones. All post IDs link to `posts/<phase>/<post-id>.md` where the
brief + prompts + caption + alt text live.

## Phase 0: Pre-launch (T-90 to T-1)

**Cadence**: 3 posts/week per platform. Total ~90 posts across 7
platforms over 12 weeks.

**Goal**: 10K email signups + 5K pre-launch followers per primary
platform (X, IG, TikTok). LinkedIn + YouTube ramp slower; target 2K
followers each.

**Weekly drumbeat** (one of each per week):

| Weekday | Theme | Post ID prefix |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | "Why we built this" - founder story or technical claim | `2026-XX-XX-mon-why-` |
| Wednesday | Device deep-dive (rotate Clip, Mini, Air, Nova week to week) | `2026-XX-XX-wed-deepdive-` |
| Friday | Community / behind-the-scenes (lab photos, calibration prints, prototype iterations) | `2026-XX-XX-fri-bts-` |

**Per-platform variation**: same theme, platform-appropriate asset.
Example for week T-12 (May 13-19 2026):

| Day | X | IG Feed | IG Reel | TikTok | LinkedIn | YouTube |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mon 5/13 9am | thread re local-first thesis | 4:5 product hero | - | - | 4:5 + technical caption | - |
| Wed 5/15 10am | quote tweet engineering deep-dive | carousel re memory tiers | 9:16 device pulse loop | 9:16 same loop + trending audio | PDF carousel re ThoxOS | - |
| Fri 5/17 11am | bts lab photo | 4:5 lifestyle | - | 9:16 timelapse | - | - |
| Sat 5/18 12pm | - | story poll re device pick | - | - | - | Short re ThoxClip print process |

**Pre-launch email signup CTA**: every post in this phase ends with
"Join the launch list at thox.ai/launch" (link in bio for IG; link
sticker for Stories; direct link in caption for X / LinkedIn / FB).

## Phase 1: Launch day (T+0 = Aug 12 2026)

**Cadence**: 8 posts across all platforms in 24 hours. Choreographed.

**Goal**: $50K in first 24h. The first 4 hours typically generate 25%
of total campaign funding; treat them like a moonshot launch.

| Hour (PT) | Platform | Post | Asset |
|---|---|---|---|
| 8:55am | All | "5 min" countdown | 1:1 video loop (countdown timer) |
| 9:00am | All | LAUNCH announcement | 4:5 + 1:1 + 9:16 hero set |
| 9:30am | IG Stories + X | "We're live!" + first backer celebration | Story sticker + reply tweet |
| 10:00am | X | thread re "what just launched" | 4-image grid |
| 11:00am | LinkedIn | full announcement post | 4:5 + technical caption |
| 12:00pm | TikTok + Reels | launch-day Reel | 9:16 video with audio |
| 1:00pm | YouTube | hero video premiere (2:30 from deliverables) | 16:9 + 9:16 Shorts cut |
| 3:00pm | Reddit | r/Kickstarter + r/LocalLLaMA posts | hero image + native text |
| 6:00pm | All | "First milestone hit" celebration | dynamic % funded graphic |

**Crisis fallback**: if the first hour does not pace toward $50K/24h
(threshold: under $5K in first hour), pause the auto-scheduled queue
and run [../runbooks/01-launch-week.md](../runbooks/01-launch-week.md)
"Underperform" branch.

## Phase 2: Week 1 (T+1 to T+7)

**Cadence**: 1-2 posts/day per platform.

**Goal**: cross $100K, unlock the first stretch goal ($250K = "All
backers get a free THOX logo coin").

**Daily template**:

| Time (PT) | Platform | Theme |
|---|---|---|
| 9am | X + IG Stories | overnight backer count + thank-you |
| 12pm | IG Reels + TikTok | device-feature spotlight (rotating Clip / Mini / Air / Nova) |
| 3pm | LinkedIn | engineering deep-dive (1 per week, rotates) |
| 6pm | X | "today in numbers" - backers, % funded, stretch progress |
| 8pm | IG Stories | "what's in tomorrow" teaser |

## Phase 3: Mid-campaign (T+8 to T+21)

**Cadence**: 1 post/day per platform. Drumbeat, not push.

**Goal**: maintain $5K/day baseline. Stretch goal progress visible
to keep momentum.

**Weekly rotation**:

| Week of campaign | Monday | Tuesday | Wednesday | Thursday | Friday | Saturday | Sunday |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Week 2 | Backer story | Device close-up | Stretch progress | Technical deep-dive | Behind the scenes | Community highlight | Press hit recap |
| Week 3 | Backer story | Device comparison | Stretch progress | Roadmap teaser | BTS shop walkthrough | UGC repost | Press hit recap |

## Phase 4: Stretch (T+22 to T+28)

**Cadence**: 2 posts/day per platform.

**Goal**: unlock $1M and push toward $2M.

**Daily template**:

| Time (PT) | Platform | Theme |
|---|---|---|
| 9am | All | "X to go until $1M" countdown |
| 12pm | TikTok + Reels | "What you unlock at $1M" reveal |
| 3pm | LinkedIn | partner / community ambassador shoutout |
| 6pm | X + IG Stories | live thermometer graphic, % funded |

## Phase 5: Final 48h (T+28 to T+30)

**Cadence**: 4 posts/day per platform. Hourly during last 6h.

**Goal**: 25-40% of campaign total in last 48h (typical Kickstarter
closeout curve).

**Final 6 hours**: hourly post on every platform with the live
countdown graphic + "last chance" language. Reply to every comment
within 15 minutes.

## Phase 6: Post-funding (T+31 onward)

**Cadence**: 3 posts/week per platform. Trust + transparency.

**Themes**:
- Manufacturing milestones (PCB delivery, enclosure print runs, QA
  pass rates).
- BackerKit pivot (add-on availability, address confirmation push).
- Build logs and engineering changelogs.
- Press coverage recaps.
- Quarterly investor / backer update aggregation.

**Cadence for the first 4 weeks post-funding** is still high (1/day per
platform) to reassure backers. Drops to 3/week after T+60.
