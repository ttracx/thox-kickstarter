# Post-funding worked example: PCB arrival build log

Phase: post-funding. Date: 2026-09-25 (T+44, ~2 weeks after campaign
close). Theme: build-log credibility post. The first major
manufacturing milestone hits backer feeds to reassure them that the
money translated to motion.

## Identity

- **Post ID**: `2026-09-25-pcb-arrival-build-log`
- **Phase**: post-funding
- **Platform(s)**: instagram (Feed + Stories), x, linkedin, youtube (Short)
- **Date / time (PT)**: 2026-09-25 11:00
- **Authored by**: Craig (hardware lead is the credible storyteller for this one)
- **Status**: ready-to-schedule

## Intent

Backer trust. The "money to motion" post type is what keeps backers
patient through the fulfillment window and keeps the BackerKit
add-on funnel converting. Secondary: justify upgrade-tier add-ons
("if the engineering is this far along, I'll add the keychain
accessory").

## Hero device

The actual PCB. Lifestyle photo of the bare PCB in-hand or on a
bench, with the THOX wordmark silk-screened visible.

## Prompts

For the PCB lifestyle shot specifically: **DO NOT GENERATE A
SYNTHETIC PCB**. Real backers reverse-image-search build-log posts.
This asset must be a real photograph.

The generation pipeline applies to the **promotional treatment** of
the real photo: dark navy backdrop replacement (composite the real PCB
photo onto the brand-aligned backdrop generated by the OpenAI
pipeline), emerald rim light grading, brand-aligned color treatment.

If the photo is captured against an off-brand background, regenerate
the background via a 1024 x 1536 OpenAI prompt for "deep navy #0a0e14
seamless studio sweep, three-point studio lighting, mirror-finish
surface reflection 30 percent opacity" and composite the real PCB
onto it in post.
