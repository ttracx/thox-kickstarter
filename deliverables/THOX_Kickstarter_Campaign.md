## The pitch in one paragraph

Most AI tools ask you to send your work, notes, files, and ideas somewhere else. THOX.ai is a
local-first hardware family for people who want private AI they can own, carry, and control: a USB
key that holds your private-AI identity, a low-cost wireless companion, a compact local compute
node, and a premium clip-on capture device. Each one is honest about what it can do. Small devices
carry identity, route commands, and capture context; heavier work routes to capable local hardware
the user owns. They speak the same THOX agent protocol. They are yours.

## The four devices

| Device | What it is | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **ThoxKey** | USB private-AI identity device | Encrypted launcher, recovery profile, and portable configuration. The simplest way into THOX.ai. |
| **ThoxMini Air** | Low-cost wireless companion | Connects nearby THOX devices and routes lightweight commands. The accessible sub-$100 node. |
| **ThoxMini** | Compact local compute node | Local services, lightweight agents, encrypted storage, and private automations for desk, lab, and field. |
| **ThoxClip** | Premium clip-on capture + command | Fast voice capture, workflow triggers, and secure handoff into your THOX environment. |

Buy one. Buy two. Buy the family. Each device does what it can honestly do and routes the rest to
hardware you own.

## Why now

AI is becoming part of every workflow, but the most valuable work is often the most sensitive:
client notes, research, strategy, code, legal drafts, product ideas, personal conversations. We
believe people should be able to use AI without giving up control of their data by default. This
campaign funds the first coordinated production run of the four launch devices and the firmware,
QA, and compliance work to ship them to backers who want private AI ownership from day one.

### Risks and what we are doing about them

- **Supply chain**: We keep the first product set focused and validate each device independently,
  with alternate suppliers identified for critical parts so a single-source disruption means a BOM
  swap and a short slip, not a cancelled reward.
- **Firmware and security**: The THOX local-first runtime is already in private alpha. The
  remaining work is device integration and hardening, not language-model R&D.
- **No LLM overclaim**: We never tell you a small device runs a chat-class LLM. ThoxKey, ThoxMini
  Air, and ThoxMini carry identity, route, and run lightweight local work; heavier work routes to
  capable local hardware the user owns. The campaign is honest about which device does which job.
- **Delivery honesty**: We use conservative delivery windows. If a date slips, backers get a direct
  update explaining what changed, why, what is next, and when the next update arrives.

## Per-device deep dives

### ThoxKey - The private-AI key

A USB device that carries your THOX identity, encrypted configuration, recovery profile, and
portable launcher. Plug it into a trusted machine and your private AI workspace opens with your
profile and settings.

**Use it for**: carrying your THOX identity, unlocking your workspace on trusted machines,
restoring settings after a rebuild, keeping launch and recovery workflows portable.

**Honest boundary**: ThoxKey is not a standalone LLM computer, a cloud subscription, or a generic
storage stick marketed as AI. It carries and unlocks the workflow; heavier work routes to capable
local hardware the user owns.

### ThoxMini Air - The wireless companion

The low-cost wireless companion for local-first AI workflows. It connects nearby THOX devices,
routes lightweight commands, and helps bridge your private workspace without requiring a cloud
account by default.

**Use it for**: lightweight local routing, wireless companion workflows, simple device-to-device
coordination, experimentation with private-AI edge setups.

**Honest boundary**: ThoxMini Air is an edge companion, not a high-end local LLM workstation.

### ThoxMini - The compact local compute node

The compact local compute node for desk, lab, and field workflows, built for local services,
lightweight agents, encrypted storage, and user-owned automations.

**Use it for**: lightweight local agents, encrypted local services, personal automations, private
workflow experiments, developer and maker workflows.

**Honest boundary**: ThoxMini is not a promise that every large model runs on tiny hardware, and it
is not a locked appliance. Large-model workloads route to capable local machines or THOX nodes.

### ThoxClip - The premium capture + command device

The premium clip-on command and capture device for private AI workflows, designed for quick voice
capture, workflow triggers, field and meeting notes, and secure handoff into your THOX environment.

**Use it for**: quick capture, private notes, workflow triggers, field and meeting context, routing
captured context to your THOX stack.

**Honest boundary**: ThoxClip is not a medical, emergency, surveillance, or regulated safety device.
