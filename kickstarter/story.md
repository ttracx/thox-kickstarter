## 04 — MeshStack

### The private network that ties it together.

MeshStack pairs every THOX device into a private mesh on your LAN. Pairing uses the WireGuard protocol. After pairing, everything works offline. The coordinator never sees plaintext. It can't read what it can't see.

- **PAIR** — One tap from ThoxOS. Devices find each other on your network.
- **ROUTE** — Small devices hand heavy work to the strongest node you own.
- **OWN** — Data stays on your devices. Your mesh, your rules.

_12 months of MeshStack Pro included with every hardware pledge._

**The MeshStack app** pairs your THOX devices into this private mesh from iOS, iPad, macOS, Windows, and Android. One-time QR pairing, then your devices talk directly. Included with the campaign.

## 10 — Risks and challenges

### What could slow us down.

**Certification.** Every device needs radio certification per region. The magnetic phone companion is the longest path, which is why ThoxClip ships in Q1 2027 instead of with the others. We do not ship uncertified hardware.

**Components.** Each device has a validated alternate for its core parts, running in firmware today. If a part goes short, we swap without a redesign.

**Dates.** Ship windows are firm intent, not contracts. If something real blocks us, you hear about it in a backer update before the date slips, with the plan to fix it.

## 12 — FAQ

**Does THOX work without the internet?**
Yes. After pairing, everything runs on your LAN. The internet is only for firmware updates, and those are opt-in.

**Can I bring my own model?**
Yes. Devices ship with a default local model set, and ThoxOS accepts compatible open-weight model files. Swap the file, restart, done.

**Is the software open source?**
The THOX runtime is open source and the firmware configs ship with every device. If THOX disappears, your devices keep working and you can rebuild the firmware yourself.

**Does ThoxClip work without another THOX device?**
Yes, as a magnetic companion for compatible phones with the THOX apps. Richer agent flows come from pairing with another THOX device on your LAN.

**What data do you collect?**
None by default. No telemetry, no analytics, no account. ThoxOS shows every outbound request and lets you allow or deny each one.

**What is the refund and warranty policy?**
Full refund before your tier's fulfillment cutoff, minus processor fees. After delivery: 30-day returns for any reason, then a 12-month warranty for manufacturing defects.
