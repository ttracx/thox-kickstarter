## 2. Global Visual and Technical Standards

- Master format: 4K UHD, 23.976 fps, 10-bit log, 16:9. Macro and product inserts at 59.94 or 119.88 fps for slow motion.
- Framing: keep money shots center-safe so 9:16 and 1:1 crops can be pulled without reframing.
- Set: dark studio matched to #09090B, black acrylic plinths, emerald practical lighting (#10B981 / #34D399, neon #00FF88 sparingly), LED wall or 65 inch plus display running mesh animation loops.
- Audio: lav plus boom on talent, room tone at every location, VO pickups in booth after picture lock.
- Screen capture: ThoxOS Mini and the THOX.ai MeshStack app at 3840x2160, dark theme, 200 percent UI scale, notifications off, demo accounts with clean sample data only.
- Typography in post: Xolonium for headlines, name cards, and device lockups. Inter for captions and lower thirds. JetBrains Mono for terminal and telemetry overlays.
- Color rules: emerald accent system only. No purple anywhere in this package (purple is reserved for MagStack, which does not appear in this campaign). No cyan.
- Performance on screen: show relative progress visually (bars, node activity, task completion). Never show numeric benchmarks, tokens per second, TOPS, or SoC names.
- Claims: only the four approved privacy claims may appear in dialogue or captions. See the compliance section.
- Talent wardrobe: dark solid tones, no third-party logos, no fine patterns (moire). THOX tee or jacket optional.
- Kickstarter delivery: master exports MP4 H.264, 5 GB max. Project image pulls from the V13 final hero: 16:9, 1024 x 576 export plus large source master.
- Imagery honesty: hardware demo sections use working prototype footage. Turntables are styled renders of physical models and carry a small Render label. AI-generated device imagery is social art only and never appears in this package. Include the Kickstarter AI-use disclosure if AI tools materially transform any video asset.
- Review buffer: a placeholder video may hold the Kickstarter slot during review; the final master swaps in before launch once the page is otherwise complete.
