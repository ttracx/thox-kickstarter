## Capture workflow (per shot)

1. Frame per the storyboard panel and the shot `action`.
2. Confirm the **on-screen proof** is visible in-frame.
3. Deliver any locked script line verbatim (speaker column).
4. Check the **acceptance gate** before moving on — it is the pass/fail condition.
5. In the tracker: mark **Captured**, add a take/location note.
6. On review, mark **Approved** once the gate is met.
