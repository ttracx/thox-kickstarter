# Runbook 04: Final 48 hours (T+28 to T+30)

The closeout push. Goal: 25-40% of total campaign funding from
fence-sitters and last-chance backers. Cadence: 4 posts/day per
platform, hourly during last 6h. The team is exhausted; tighten
operations.

## T+28 (Tue Sep 8 2026) - "2 days left" day

### 6:00am PT - team standup

- [ ] Pull overnight numbers.
- [ ] Confirm assigned operator coverage for the next 48 hours
      (rotating 4-hour shifts, no one works more than 12 hours
      consecutively).
- [ ] Confirm reply SLA: 15 minutes for the entire 48h window.

### Posts (per platform, 4 posts/day)

| Time (PT) | Theme |
|---|---|
| 9:00am | "2 days left" master post + early-bird tier reminder |
| 12:00pm | UGC repost / backer testimonial |
| 3:00pm | "Last X% to next stretch" or "Last X tier units remaining" depending on which is closer |
| 6:00pm | "End of day" recap with funded amount + countdown |

### Daily push checklist

- [ ] Email blast at 9am PT: "Last 48 hours" email to the launch list
      (template:
      `../templates/launch-day-email.md` adapted for closeout).
- [ ] Press: send the "campaign closing in 48h" final-call email to
      the press list.
- [ ] Influencer outreach: text the top 10 launch-day influencers
      with a "campaign closes in 48h, one more post would help" ask.

## T+29 (Wed Sep 9 2026) - "Tomorrow" day

## T+30 (Thu Sep 10 2026) - close day

The most intense day of the entire campaign.

### 6:00am PT

- [ ] All-hands standup. Last day. Operators in 2-hour shifts now.
- [ ] Confirm the campaign close time: 6:00pm PT (18:00 UTC-7).
- [ ] Pre-stage the close-time celebration content.

### 6:00am - 12:00pm PT (morning push, hourly)

- [ ] Hourly thermometer post on X + IG Stories per
      `../social/posts/05-final-48h/2026-09-10-final-6h-countdown.md`
      (the brief is templated for the final-6h push but the cadence
      kicks in at noon).
- [ ] Reply window: continuous.

### 12:00pm - 6:00pm PT (final 6 hours)

THE PUSH. Hourly posts on every platform per the final-6h brief.
Caption rotation. Live thermometer graphic regenerated each hour.

| Hour | T-X | Caption emphasis |
|---|---|---|
| 12pm | T-6h | "6 hours left" |
| 1pm | T-5h | "5 hours left" |
| 2pm | T-4h | "4 hours left" + early-bird tier specific |
| 3pm | T-3h | "3 hours" + "$39 ThoxClip closes when campaign closes" |
| 4pm | T-2h | "2 hours" + funded amount |
| 5pm | T-1h | "LAST HOUR" |
| 5:45pm | T-15m | "15 minutes" |
| 5:55pm | T-5m | "5 minutes" |
| 5:59pm | T-1m | "60 seconds" |

### 6:00:00pm PT - close

- [ ] Campaign owner watches the Kickstarter creator dashboard.
- [ ] At the exact close: post the "FUNDED" celebration on all 7
      platforms within 60 seconds.
- [ ] Final funded amount + backer count + a one-sentence thank you.
- [ ] Open a fresh thread of replies to anyone who replies in the
      first 30 minutes after close.

### 6:00pm - 11:00pm PT - close-day decompression

- [ ] Reply to every comment for 5 hours after close. Backers
      remember the close-day reply more than any other reply they'll
      get all year.
- [ ] Send the "We did it" email to the backer list (template:
      `../templates/weekly-update.md` adapted for the close).
- [ ] Press: send the "Campaign closed at $X funded" final email to
      press list. Most outlets will write a closing recap if you
      provide the numbers proactively.
- [ ] Internal: team dinner / drinks. The 30 days are over. The next
      phase (fulfillment) is harder, but tonight is for celebrating.

## Close-day acceptance gate

- [ ] Campaign closed at or above $250K baseline goal
- [ ] All reply threads on launch-day, $1M unlock, and close-day
      posts answered within 24h
- [ ] Press list emailed with final numbers
- [ ] Backer email sent
- [ ] Internal team check-in: nobody is burned out beyond the next
      48 hours of recovery time

## Common closeout failure modes

| Failure | Recovery |
|---|---|
| Campaign falls short of goal | Kickstarter refunds all pledges automatically. Post a transparent "we didn't make it, here's what's next" message. Do NOT pretend it didn't happen. |
| Operator burnout mid-final-6h | Swap to backup operator immediately. The reply SLA is more important than the lead operator's pride. |
| Kickstarter site outage (rare but happens) | Screenshot the live counter at the time of outage. Post on X. Email the press list. Kickstarter usually credits "outage minutes" by extending the campaign 30-60 min; confirm with the rep. |
| Negative press hit during final 48h | See [07-crisis-response.md](07-crisis-response.md). Do not engage publicly until crisis-lead has reviewed. |
| Stretch ceiling overshot ($3M+) | Post the celebration but include a transparent "we'll stop adding tiers; fulfillment scope is locked at $3M" message. Backers respect discipline. |
