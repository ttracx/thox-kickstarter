# Partnerships

PRE-OUTREACH internal-only memos for Tommy + Craig to review BEFORE any actual partnership outreach is initiated for the THOX.ai Kickstarter launching August 12, 2026.

Nothing in this folder commits THOX.ai to any specific partner, deal, NDA, financial transaction, or public announcement. Every commitment requires Tommy + Craig joint sign-off and (above $10K or where any of the briefs flag attorney review) startup-attorney sign-off as well.

## What this folder is

Eight per-company partnership briefs plus three workflow docs. Each brief is a one-pager (roughly 600-1000 words) that summarizes why a partnership with a specific company might matter to THOX, why it might matter to the company, a mutual-fit score, specific asks THOX could make, specific offers THOX could make, risks and non-starters, recommended outreach path, recommended timing, decision authority, and disclaimers.

The briefs are conversation starters, not commitments. They do not contain executive names. They do not contain any fabricated information. They reference real THOX technical capabilities documented elsewhere in this repo and in the broader THOX portfolio (per the memory entries cited in each brief).

## What this folder is NOT

- NOT a list of partnerships THOX has signed
- NOT a list of partnerships THOX is currently negotiating
- NOT a script for cold outreach
- NOT a press release
- NOT a public marketing document
- NOT a commitment to any specific timeline, dollar value, or deal shape
- NOT legal advice

## When to use this folder

- BEFORE any partnership outreach, to align Tommy + Craig on the per-company posture
- BEFORE engaging the THOX attorney on a partnership opportunity, to give the attorney structured context
- BEFORE a partnership conversation moves from discovery to formal agreement, to revisit the original asks and offers
- BEFORE any joint marketing material is drafted, to ensure the marketing reflects what THOX actually intends

## When NOT to use this folder

- DO NOT share the per-brief files with the named company or any of their representatives. The briefs are internal-only and contain unredacted strategic framing.
- DO NOT publish any of this content on docs.thox.ai or any public surface.
- DO NOT forward to a PR firm or marketing agency without first redacting the strategic-assessment sections.

## How to read the briefs

Each brief follows a standard template:

1. **Why this partnership matters to THOX** (3 paragraphs)
2. **Why this partnership might matter to the company** (3 paragraphs)
3. **Mutual fit assessment** (1-10 scoring on strategic alignment, technical compatibility, brand fit, commercial overlap risk, weighted total)
4. **Specific asks we could make** (5-7 bullet points)
5. **What we could offer** (5-7 bullet points)
6. **Risks + non-starters** (hard "do not accept" rules)
7. **Recommended outreach path** (tier, channel, who NOT to email)
8. **Recommended timing** (T-21 to T-0 + post-launch follow-on)
9. **Decision authority** (Tommy + Craig sign-off + attorney gates)
10. **Disclaimers** (internal-only, no fabricated names, conversation starter only)
