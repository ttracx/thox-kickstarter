# Partnership brief: THOX <-> Cohere

PRE-OUTREACH MEMO. Tommy + Craig review before any contact is initiated. Internal only. Do not send. Do not share with Cohere or their representatives without redaction.

## Why this partnership matters to THOX

Cohere is the cleanest enterprise + small-model story among the Tier-1 frontier labs. Their North-Mini-Code family in particular targets the same on-device-or-near-device deployment surface THOX is building hardware for. The Cohere North-Mini-Code baseline eval is already on the THOX rig per the today's sprint, which means the integration work is not theoretical: THOXCore's router already understands the shape of a North-Mini-Code response. A partnership formalizes a working integration that exists at the byte level today.

Operationally, Cohere fills a real gap in THOX's cloud-tier story. Anthropic and OpenAI are the obvious general-purpose adapters. Cohere is the obvious code-assist adapter for developer-segment THOX buyers who want code suggestions to route to a model that was trained for the job. The THOXKey EDU SKU in particular is a natural fit because the typical curriculum buyer wants a code-friendly tier that is not the same brand the students already use in browser-based ChatGPT.

Strategically, Cohere is the most reciprocal partnership of the four Tier-1 candidates. Cohere needs developer mindshare. THOX has developer mindshare in the maker + privacy + edge-AI segment that Cohere does not currently reach. The trade is real: THOX gets a credible code-tier adapter at preferred pricing, Cohere gets distribution into a developer audience they otherwise pay LinkedIn ads to reach.

## Why this partnership might matter to Cohere

Cohere is in an active fight for mindshare against the much larger OpenAI and Anthropic brands. Every distribution channel that does not require a paid acquisition spend is valuable to Cohere right now. THOX is a free distribution channel: every THOX device shipped with the Cohere adapter pre-configured is a developer or prosumer who tries North-Mini-Code without a paid acquisition cost.

THOX also brings a deployment pattern that Cohere has been pushing in their enterprise messaging: on-premises and at-the-edge AI for regulated industries. THOX is the edge-side of that story. A "THOX device on the desk plus Cohere in your VPC" reference architecture is something Cohere can put in front of healthcare, legal, government, and financial-services buyers immediately. THOX validates the edge tier of that story without Cohere having to build hardware themselves.

Finally, the Cohere brand voice and the THOX brand voice rhyme. Both are technical-but-honest. Both are skeptical of hype. Both ship things that work. Co-marketing copy writes itself when the two voices align, which is rare in the AI infrastructure space.

- Strategic alignment: 8 (Cohere targets enterprise + on-prem + regulated verticals; THOX brings the edge tier of that story)
- Technical compatibility: 9 (North-Mini-Code eval already on rig; adapter exists at the byte level; trivial to formalize)
- Brand fit: 9 (both technical-but-honest; both ship; both skeptical of hype)
- Commercial overlap risk: 1 (Cohere does not sell hardware; THOX does not run a hosted-API business; zero direct overlap)
- Total weighted score: 8.5
