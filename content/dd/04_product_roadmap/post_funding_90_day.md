## Day 0 to 30 - close-out and capture

- Final pledge count and revenue captured per SKU.
- Backer survey emitted (address capture, SKU configuration, add-ons).
- Survey response rate target: 80% by day 14, 95% by day 30.
- Bank account routing finalized for Kickstarter payout.
- Supplier deposits sized against committed unit volumes.
- Press follow-up cadence per docs/PRESS_KIT.md.

## Day 31 to 60 - manufacturing and inbound

- Component POs issued.
- Assembly line set up per content/manuals/ and thox-quickstart boms/.
- Pilot batch (FILL: units) produced and shipped to internal QA.
- Pilot-batch defect findings folded back into the assembly walkthrough.

## Day 61 to 90 - first wave fulfillment

- First customer wave shipped (FILL: target units per SKU).
- Outbound shipping carrier locked.
- Backer support inbox staffed (cross-link customer-support skill flow).
- Returns and warranty process operational.

## Risks

Cross-link docs/RISKS.md. Top three for the 90-day window:

1. Component lead time on Luckfox Pico Mini B for ThoxClip / Mini / Air.
2. LattePanda N100 supply for ThoxNova (sole-source dependency).
3. Fulfillment carrier dependency for international destinations.
