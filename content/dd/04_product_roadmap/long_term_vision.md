# Long-term vision (18-month outlook)

Last updated: 2026-06-25
Status: DRAFT - directional, not commitments

---

## Eighteen-month horizon

THOX.ai aims to be the canonical on-device AI platform for personal,
small-business, and educational contexts where data sovereignty and
offline operation are non-negotiable.

### Product directions

- Continued hardware family expansion: ThoxClip, ThoxMini, ThoxAir,
  ThoxNova as the first wave; ThoxVault, ThoxCargo, ThoxArm in the next
  wave per ttracx/thox-playbooks.
- Continued model family expansion: ThoxMicro (125M) and ThoxLLM (327M)
  baselines; ThoxGemma SFT family per Phase B/C; thoxllm-factory
  ten-model lineup continuing to ship monthly.
- ThoxOS as the supervisory layer that ties devices into a single mesh.
- THOX Experience Fabric as the cross-platform UX layer with codegen
  for SwiftUI, Compose, and Rust.

### Distribution directions

- Direct-to-consumer via Thox.ai marketing site.
- Educational channel via EDU pricing letter.
- Selected B2B integrations via partnership briefs (see 10_partnerships).
- Selected reseller relationships per supply availability.

### Community directions

- thox-meta governance footprint applied across the contributor base.
- Documentation site (thox-docs) expanded into a tutorials + reference
  hub for the on-device AI developer ecosystem.

## Out of scope at 18 months

- Multi-tenant cloud LLM hosting (not the THOX thesis).
- General-purpose laptop or smartphone OEM business.
- Cryptocurrency or token issuance of any kind.

## Disclaimer

Forward-looking. Reflects current intent. Subject to change based on
campaign outcomes, supply chain, partner availability, and market
response. Not a commitment to deliver any specific product on any
specific date.
