## THOX differentiation thesis

- On-device by default. Offline-first. Data does not leave the device
  unless the user opts in.
- Affordable consumer price points (ThoxClip $39 baseline; ThoxNova
  $499 ceiling per current playbook).
- Open-source platform under Apache-2.0 with proprietary brand,
  manufacturing, and trained-model assets.
- Multi-device mesh (MagStack clustering) - no current competitor offers
  this in the consumer band.
