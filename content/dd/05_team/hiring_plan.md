## Trigger-based hires

Hires are gated by Kickstarter and follow-on round outcomes, not by date.

### Tier 1 - baseline campaign ($250K)

- 1 fulfillment / operations contractor (part-time, FILL: monthly cost).
- 1 customer-support contractor (part-time, FILL: monthly cost).
- Founders continue to cover engineering, product, BD.

### Tier 2 - mid ($1M)

- 1 full-time hardware engineer.
- 1 full-time firmware / embedded engineer.
- 1 full-time operations lead (own fulfillment + supplier mgmt).
- Continue founder coverage of product + BD + community.

### Tier 3 - ceiling ($3M)

- Add: 1 full-time ML engineer (model fine-tuning + evals).
- Add: 1 full-time community / developer-relations lead.
- Add: 1 full-time marketing lead.
- Add: 1 full-time customer-success lead.
- Convert support and ops contractors to FTE if performance warrants.
