# Privacy posture

Last updated: 2026-06-25
Status: DRAFT - attorney refinement required

---

## Posture summary

- On-device by default. Personal data does not leave the device unless
  the user explicitly opts in.
- Kickstarter platform holds backer data per Kickstarter ToS.
- Email used for fulfillment, support, and product-update comms only.
- No third-party data sale.

## Regulatory frameworks of interest

- EU GDPR (any backer in EU territory).
- UK GDPR.
- California CCPA / CPRA.
- Virginia VCDPA.
- Colorado CPA.
- Connecticut CTDPA.
- Utah UCPA.
- Texas TDPSA (relevant if Cedar Park TX is the state of formation).

## Data lifecycle (planned)

- Collect: email (account), address (fulfillment), SKU configuration
  (fulfillment). Optional account preferences post-purchase.
- Process: limited to fulfillment, support, and product updates.
- Retain: per attorney-set schedule. Default retention not yet set.
- Delete: on user request via standard data-subject-access flow.

## Subject rights

- Access, correction, deletion, portability per applicable framework.
- Single contact: privacy@thox.ai (FILL: confirm mailbox provisioned).
- Response timelines per applicable framework.

## Disclaimer

This document is a starting framework. Final privacy policy must be
attorney-drafted and published on Thox.ai before campaign open.
