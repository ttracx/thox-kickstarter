# INVESTOR_OUTREACH

Internal preparation pack for the THOX.ai LLC pre-seed/seed round that
closes alongside or shortly after the Aug 12 2026 Kickstarter campaign.

Brand: #0B1220 / #F2F4F8 / #27E5FF / #FF3DA8. IBM Plex Sans + JetBrains
Mono.

Voice: technical, honest, no marketing fluff. No fabricated investor
names. No fabricated contact info. No commitments to or from any
investor.

---

## What this pack is

Four working docs the operator uses to convert "we want to raise a round"
into a managed pipeline of warm-introduced, screened-in conversations
with category-fit investors.

This pack is companion material to the public-facing deck:

- `../INVESTOR_DECK.md` (10-slide markdown source)
- `../INVESTOR_DECK.pdf` (rendered, 10 slides + speaker notes)
- `../INVESTOR_DECK_ONE_PAGER.md` (single-page markdown source)
- `../INVESTOR_DECK_ONE_PAGER.pdf` (rendered, 1 page)

The deck and one-pager go to investors. This pack stays internal.

---

## Workflow

Use the docs in this order:

1. **`INVESTOR_TARGET_LIST.md`** -- 4-tier investor profile (edge-AI /
   privacy-tech specialists, hardware + community-funded crossover, AI +
   open-source / community-aligned, strategic + corporate VC). Tiers are
   profiled by FUND THESIS, not specific names. The operator verifies
   thesis match before adding any specific firm to the private tracker.

2. **`INVESTOR_SCREENING_FRAMEWORK.md`** -- 6-question screening with
   scores 0 to 5 per question (max 30). Green at 25+, yellow at 18 to 24,
   red below 18 or on any hard-disqualify answer.

3. **`WARM_INTRO_BUILDER.md`** -- how to build a warm-intro path to a
   screened-in candidate. Founder network, operator / advisor network,
   community channels, "ask a friend who's raised before." LinkedIn 2nd-
   degree heuristic. X / Twitter bio scrape for thesis signal (NOT for
   cold-DM targeting). Forwardable intro template.

4. **`PITCH_MEETING_PLAYBOOK.md`** -- 15-min discovery / 30-min deep-dive
   / 60-min hot-seat formats. What to send in advance (one-pager only,
   not the full deck). What to ask the investor. How to follow up
   within 24 hours. 7 common objections + responses. 10 term-sheet red
   flags requiring attorney consultation.

## What this pack does NOT contain

- Specific fund names recommended for outreach. The operator verifies
  thesis match per `INVESTOR_TARGET_LIST.md` Section "How to use this
  list," then adds specific names to a private CRM-lite tracker outside
  this repo.

- Specific partner names. The operator identifies the right partner per
  firm via the firm's website and LinkedIn per
  `WARM_INTRO_BUILDER.md` Step 1.

- Specific contact info (email, phone, DM handles) for any investor.
  Cold contact via any channel is prohibited per
  `WARM_INTRO_BUILDER.md` Core Rule. All first-touch must come through
  a warm intro from a third party.

- Commitments to or from any investor. Nothing in this pack obligates
  THOX or any investor to anything.

- Dollar figures for the round. The Ask section of the deck remains
  user-set; Tommy and Craig jointly finalize amount, equity, and pre-
  money before any external send.

- Legal advice. All term-sheet review must come from THOX's startup
  attorney. All tax advice on round structure (QSBS, 83(b), pre-money
  valuation) must come from THOX's CPA.
