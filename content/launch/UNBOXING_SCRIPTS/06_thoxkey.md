# THOXKey unboxing - 30s social cut

## Posting plan

- Channel: Instagram Reels + TikTok + X
- Date: T-21 days (July 22, 2026), same day as `01_thoxclip.md`
- Founder voice: Tommy leads (the THOXKey is the software-keyring
  swag); Craig delivers the closing CTA
- Render dependencies: THOXKey iso hero render
- Position in cadence: this is the small-swag tier; pairs with the
  ThoxClip cut for a "two-thing day"

## Script (30s)

### Beat 1 (0 to 5s): Hook

On camera: a small THOX mailer envelope on a dark walnut desk.
Brand-cyan rim light from camera-right. Hands enter from frame-bottom.

Voiceover (Tommy): "This is THOXKey. The smallest thing we ship."

### Beat 2 (5 to 13s): Reveal

On camera: hands open the mailer; the THOXKey slides out into the
palm. It is a USB-A keychain stick with the THOX wordmark debossed
into the body and a small loop for a keyring.

Voiceover (Tommy): "USB-A. Wordmark deboss. Keyring loop. Forty-eight
millimeters end to end."

### Beat 3 (13 to 22s): Plug-in moment

On camera: hands plug the THOXKey into a laptop USB-A port. A small
overlay fades in on the laptop screen showing the THOXKey contents:
the THOX system prompts, the device profile catalog, and a few
starter skills.

Voiceover (Tommy): "Carries the THOX system prompts, the device
profile catalog, and starter skills. Read-only. No telemetry."

### Beat 4 (22 to 30s): CTA

On camera: cut to the THOX logo overlay over a clean macro of the
THOXKey on the keyring. Kickstarter URL under the logo.

Voiceover (Craig): "Add-on tier. Back us on Kickstarter. August
twelve."

## Music

- Driving electronic bed, 95 to 105 BPM, no vocals. Lighter than the
  device cuts because the THOXKey is an add-on, not a primary SKU.
- Cyan-light sync points at 5 s, 13 s, and 22 s.
- Drop the bed for the final 1.5 s after the CTA voiceover.

## Compliance

- No medical claims.
- No financial claims.
- No competitor names visible or spoken.
- "Read-only. No telemetry." must match the THOXKey shipping image.
  If the shipping image gains a writable partition or any telemetry,
  cut both lines.
- The THOXKey is an add-on tier, not a primary SKU. The CTA in this
  cut intentionally does not call a price out loud. The add-on price
  appears in the rewards page only.
- "August twelve" verbal claim verifiable against the Kickstarter URL.
