# ThoxClip unboxing - 30s social cut

## Posting plan

- Channel: Instagram Reels + TikTok + X
- Date: T-21 days (July 22, 2026)
- Founder voice: Craig (hardware) leads; Tommy delivers the closing CTA
- Render dependencies: ThoxClip iso hero render and wordmark macro
- Sibling cut: this is paired with `06_thoxkey.md` in the same week

## Script (30s)

### Beat 1 (0 to 5s): Hook

On camera: a sealed THOX shipping mailer on a dark walnut desk. Brand-cyan
rim light from camera-right. Two hands enter from frame-bottom and lift
the mailer flap.

Voiceover (Craig): "This is ThoxClip. Pocket-scale private AI."

### Beat 2 (5 to 13s): Reveal

On camera: hands tip the mailer; the ThoxClip slides out into a foam
cradle. Macro on the clip body, THOX wordmark legible.

Voiceover (Craig): "Matte black body. Real magnetic catch. Clips to a
lapel, a strap, or a bag."

### Beat 3 (13 to 22s): Clip-on motion

On camera: right hand picks up the ThoxClip; left hand brings up a
jacket lapel; the clip snaps closed with a real Foley snap. Indicator
LED pulses cyan once.

Voiceover (Craig): "Snap it on. The indicator pulses cyan. You are
running."

### Beat 4 (22 to 30s): CTA

On camera: cut to the THOX logo overlay over a clean shot of the clip
on the lapel. Kickstarter URL under the logo.

Voiceover (Tommy): "From thirty-nine dollars. Back us on Kickstarter.
August twelve."

## Shot list

1. Sealed mailer, low key light + cyan rim, 5 s.
2. Mailer flap lift, macro on the THOX wordmark on the mailer, 3 s.
3. ThoxClip sliding into the foam cradle, top-down 4K macro, 3 s.
4. ThoxClip body macro, focus pull from clip side to wordmark, 4 s.
5. Hand picks up ThoxClip, side angle, 2 s.
6. Jacket lapel rises into frame, 2 s.
7. Clip-on snap close-up at 240 fps slow-mo, 4 s real time.
8. Indicator LED pulse close-up, 2 s.
9. Logo overlay + CTA, 5 s.
