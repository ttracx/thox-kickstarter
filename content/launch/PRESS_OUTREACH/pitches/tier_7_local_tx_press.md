# tier_7_local_tx_press.md

Local Texas / Austin metro press. THOX.ai operations are in Cedar Park (Austin metro). Local press coverage is the highest-conversion press for community + recruiting + early backers in the home market.

**Lead angle for this tier**: founder-led from Texas; Cedar Park-based; shipping hardware globally from the Austin metro.
**Recommended template sequence**: initial_outreach -> interview_offer -> follow_up_3_day.

## Embargo policy

Local press is generally embargo-friendly when terms are documented. Pitch at T-3 to T-7.

## Outlets

---

### Austin American-Statesman

- **URL**: https://www.statesman.com
- **Beat**: Austin metro daily newspaper. Strong business and tech sections covering Austin's tech ecosystem.
- **Pitch angle**: Cedar Park founders launching globally on Kickstarter. The Austin tech ecosystem angle: open-source hardware built in the metro area. If either founder has a local backstory (school, prior employer, community ties), surface that.
- **Contact channel**: `news@statesman.com` for general news tips. The Statesman has a business desk; pull the byline from a recent local tech-startup story for a reporter-specific pitch.
- **Submission guidelines**: https://www.statesman.com/contact-us/ (verify)
- **Lead time**: 3-7 days.
- **Embargo policy**: respects embargoes.
- **Coverage history**: Austin tech startups, local Kickstarter campaigns, AI companies in the metro.

---

### Austin Business Journal

- **URL**: https://www.bizjournals.com/austin
- **Beat**: Austin business community. Strong on startups, funding, real estate, M&A. Subscription publication with high local-leadership reach.
- **Pitch angle**: bootstrapped Austin metro hardware company. Funding model (Kickstarter), founder bios, headcount, hiring plans. ABJ readers are business leaders who may become customers, partners, or hires.
- **Contact channel**: ABJ has a published news tips channel. Reporter-specific: pull byline from recent local-tech-startup or Kickstarter coverage. ABJ has a dedicated tech reporter.
- **Submission guidelines**: https://www.bizjournals.com/austin/contactus (verify)
- **Lead time**: 5-10 days.
- **Embargo policy**: respects embargoes.
- **Coverage history**: Austin tech startups, funding announcements, founder profiles.

---

### Texas Monthly

- **URL**: https://www.texasmonthly.com
- **Beat**: Texas-wide magazine. Long-form features, culture, business, politics. Feature-driven, not news-hit driven.
- **Pitch angle**: Texas-founders-build-hardware story. Texas Monthly cares about culture and craft; the angle is "two Texas engineers shipping AI hardware in the open." Texas Monthly does not do product launch news; they do feature stories about Texans doing notable work.
- **Contact channel**: Texas Monthly has a published contact page. For features, pitch the relevant section editor (business / tech).
- **Submission guidelines**: https://www.texasmonthly.com/about-us/contact/ (verify)
- **Lead time**: 30+ days. Texas Monthly is a monthly with a long calendar.
- **Embargo policy**: respects embargoes; less relevant for feature timing.
- **Coverage history**: feature stories on Texas tech founders, makers, entrepreneurs.

---

### KUT (Austin NPR member station)

- **URL**: https://www.kut.org
- **Beat**: Austin public radio. Tech coverage on the Texas Standard and local news. Audio-first format.
- **Pitch angle**: founder interview for a tech segment. Local angle is the entry point. KUT will value the open-source and public-good framing.
- **Contact channel**: KUT has a published newsroom contact. Reporter-specific: pull from recent tech coverage.
- **Submission guidelines**: https://www.kut.org/about (verify contact channel)
- **Lead time**: 7-14 days for a scheduled segment.
- **Embargo policy**: less relevant for radio interview format.
- **Coverage history**: local tech founders, Austin tech ecosystem coverage.

---

## Common dos and don'ts for Tier 7

- **Do**: emphasize the Cedar Park / Austin metro location. Local press cares about the local angle.
- **Do**: offer in-person interview availability. Founders are local to Austin metro; this is a competitive advantage versus remote pitches.
- **Do**: provide local-relevant photography (the print rig in the workshop, the founders at a Cedar Park or Austin location).
- **Don't**: pitch all four outlets in the same day. Local press communities talk; staggered outreach reads better.
- **Don't**: treat KUT like a press release pipeline. They want a conversation.
