## Why this order

ThoxClip first because it has the smallest BOM and the most repeatable assembly. ThoxNova last because the LattePanda N100 has the longest lead time and ThoxNova owners can use any laptop in the meantime; nothing is gated on it shipping first.

Family Bundle and Founders Pack last because they require all four devices to be in stock simultaneously.

## What slips look like

If we slip on any reward by more than 30 days, we send:
- An Update on Kickstarter with the new date and the root cause
- An email to affected backers with a refund offer (full refund for the slipped tier, regardless of fault)
- A status video showing the part of the line that is delayed

If we slip by more than 90 days on any tier, we add a $20 thoxy_credit to the affected backer's BackerKit account for use on add-ons or future merch.
