# Bookstore Channel Waiver Letter (Template)

> **DISCLAIMER**: General guidance, not legal advice. THOX.ai LLC must have a qualified attorney review and customize this template before signing or sending. Square brackets `[...]` indicate fields the operator fills in. Text in `<<...>>` indicates editorial notes for the operator or attorney to remove before execution. This template waives standard channel terms (ISBN requirement, distributor markup, returns policy) for university bookstore and authorized educational reseller channels, in exchange for the channel adhering to THOX-specified pricing, brand handling, and resale conditions.

**Re: Waiver of Standard Channel Terms - THOX EDU Bookstore Program**

Dear [CONTACT NAME]:

This letter (this "**Waiver Letter**") confirms the waivers and modifications that THOX.ai LLC ("**THOX**") is extending to [BOOKSTORE / RESELLER LEGAL NAME] (the "**Channel**") in connection with the Channel's resale of THOX hardware and software products (the "**Products**") to students, faculty, and authorized program participants at [INSTITUTION NAME OR PROGRAM] (the "**Institution**").

This Waiver Letter supplements (and to the extent specified, modifies) the Bulk Order Agreement separately executed between THOX and the Channel (the "**Bulk Order Agreement**"). All capitalized terms not defined here have the meanings given in the Bulk Order Agreement.

## 1. Waiver of ISBN requirement

The Channel typically requires an ISBN for any item carried in the bookstore inventory system. THOX hardware Products are not published works and therefore do not have an ISBN. THOX hereby authorizes the Channel to use the THOX internal SKU (set forth in the Bulk Order Agreement Schedule A) in lieu of an ISBN for inventory, point-of-sale, and reporting purposes. Where the Channel's internal systems require a numeric placeholder, the Channel may use the SKU's numeric component or the GTIN/UPC THOX prints on the Product packaging.

THOX represents that the SKUs are unique within the THOX product line. The Channel is not required to obtain an ISBN for the Products.

## 2. Waiver of standard distributor markup

The Channel typically applies a standard markup to all inventory. For the THOX EDU Program, the Channel agrees to apply a maximum markup of [TEN PERCENT (10%)] over the EDU Price set forth in the Bulk Order Agreement Schedule A. The resulting price (the "**Channel Resale Price**") may not exceed the THOX MSRP for the same Product.

Example: if the EDU Price is $50 and the THOX MSRP is $69, the Channel Resale Price may be up to $55 (a 10% markup over EDU Price, well below MSRP). If the EDU Price is $63 and the MSRP is $69, the Channel Resale Price may be up to $69 (the MSRP cap binds before the 10% markup).

The Channel may discount below the Channel Resale Price at its discretion. The Channel may not exceed the Channel Resale Price without THOX's prior written consent.

## 3. Returns policy waiver

The Channel's standard returns policy requires THOX to accept returns of unsold inventory at the end of each academic term. THOX hereby waives that requirement and instead extends the following modified returns terms:

- **Defective units**: returnable to THOX within [THIRTY (30)] days of receipt by the Channel for full credit or replacement, per the warranty terms in the Bulk Order Agreement.
- **Unsold inventory**: not returnable to THOX. THOX recommends the Channel order in quantities that match enrollment, with a 5-10% buffer for breakage. THOX will work with the Channel each academic year to refine forecast.
- **Damaged-in-shipment**: returnable to THOX within [FIFTEEN (15)] days of receipt for full credit or replacement, with photographic evidence of the damage.

This Section 3 supersedes any return-on-unsold-inventory provision in the Channel's standard purchase terms.

## 4. Resale conditions

The Channel agrees that, in exchange for the waivers in Sections 1 through 3:

- (a) the Products will be sold only to students, faculty, staff, and program participants of the Institution, identified by valid institutional ID at the point of sale;
- (b) the Channel will not resell the Products through any third-party marketplace (Amazon, eBay, Walmart Marketplace) without THOX's prior written consent;
- (c) the Channel will display the Products with the THOX-provided marketing materials (signage, shelf talkers, packaging) and will not modify or remove THOX trademarks;
- (d) the Channel will pass through to the end purchaser the THOX warranty, support contact, and Privacy Policy disclosures included with each Product;
- (e) the Channel will report to THOX, on a [MONTHLY / QUARTERLY] cadence, the quantity of each SKU sold and the institution-affiliated identifier of each purchaser (or aggregate counts where individual identification is restricted by privacy law);
- (f) the Channel will not bundle the Products with any non-THOX product, software, or service without THOX's prior written consent; and
- (g) the Channel will direct any warranty, technical support, or product question from the end purchaser to THOX support at [URL] or [EMAIL], not to the Channel's own customer service.

## 5. Marketing co-op (optional)

THOX will provide the Channel with [DOLLAR AMOUNT] per quarter in marketing co-op funds, redeemable for THOX-approved marketing activities including: in-store displays, student newspaper advertisements, instructor outreach mailings, and the Institution's continuing-education catalog placements. Co-op funds expire at the end of each calendar quarter and do not roll over. The Channel will submit receipts and a brief activity report to claim co-op reimbursement.

## 6. FERPA and student privacy

The Channel will not share student-level purchase data with THOX in a manner that would violate the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), 20 U.S.C. Section 1232g, or any state-equivalent law. Aggregate counts (e.g., "47 ThoxClips sold in fall semester to enrolled students of course CS-301") are acceptable. Per-student identifiable purchase records should not be shared with THOX unless the student has explicitly consented and the disclosure complies with FERPA.

## 7. Term

This Waiver Letter is in effect for the [ACADEMIC YEAR / PERIOD], beginning [START DATE] and ending [END DATE]. The Waiver Letter automatically renews for successive academic years on the same terms, unless either Party gives written notice of non-renewal at least [SIXTY (60)] days before the end of the then-current term.

## 8. Termination

THOX may terminate this Waiver Letter on [THIRTY (30)] days written notice to the Channel for any reason. Termination of this Waiver Letter does not terminate the Bulk Order Agreement, which continues in accordance with its own terms.

## 9. Compliance with the Bulk Order Agreement

All other terms of the Bulk Order Agreement remain in full force and effect. In the event of any conflict between this Waiver Letter and the Bulk Order Agreement, this Waiver Letter controls for the matters expressly addressed herein.

## 10. No further waivers

THOX's grant of the waivers in Sections 1 through 3 is limited to the specific waivers set forth and does not constitute a waiver of any other THOX rights, remedies, or terms.
