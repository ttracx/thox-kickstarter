# Vendor / Bulk Order Agreement (Template)

> **DISCLAIMER**: General guidance, not legal advice. THOX.ai LLC must have a qualified attorney review and customize this template before signing or sending. Square brackets `[...]` indicate fields the operator fills in. Text in `<<...>>` indicates editorial notes for the operator or attorney to remove before execution. This template covers B2B bulk orders (channel partner, reseller, EDU bookstore, corporate buyer) and component supply (PCB, mechanical, packaging). The operator and attorney should fork into two specialized templates if both use cases are active.

**VENDOR AND BULK ORDER AGREEMENT**

This Vendor and Bulk Order Agreement (this "**Agreement**") is entered into as of [DATE] (the "**Effective Date**") by and between:

**THOX.ai LLC**, a [Nevada / Texas] limited liability company with its principal place of business at [STREET], [CITY], [STATE] [ZIP] ("**THOX**"); and

**[COUNTERPARTY LEGAL NAME]**, a [STATE] [ENTITY TYPE] with its principal place of business at [STREET], [CITY], [STATE] [ZIP] ("**Vendor**").

THOX and Vendor are each a "**Party**" and collectively the "**Parties**".

## 1. Definitions

**"Products"** means the THOX hardware devices, software, and accessories listed in **Schedule A** (Product List and Pricing).

**"Purchase Order"** or **"PO"** means a written or electronic order issued by Vendor to THOX referencing this Agreement and specifying Products, quantities, prices, delivery dates, and ship-to address.

**"Specifications"** means the technical specifications for the Products as published by THOX from time to time, including any datasheets, user manuals, and acceptance criteria.

**"Territory"** means [GLOBAL / UNITED STATES / NORTH AMERICA / SPECIFIC COUNTRIES].

**"Term"** has the meaning set forth in Section 11.

## 2. Engagement; Scope

Subject to the terms of this Agreement, THOX appoints Vendor as a [non-exclusive / exclusive] [reseller / distributor / channel partner / educational reseller / corporate buyer] of the Products in the Territory during the Term. Vendor accepts the appointment and agrees to use commercially reasonable efforts to promote and resell the Products in accordance with this Agreement.

## 3. Purchase orders; acceptance

3.1 **PO submission**. Vendor submits POs through [EMAIL / PORTAL / ERP INTEGRATION]. Each PO must reference this Agreement and include: (a) Product SKU, (b) quantity, (c) unit price (from Schedule A), (d) requested ship date, (e) ship-to address, and (f) Vendor's PO number.

3.2 **Acceptance**. THOX accepts a PO by written acknowledgment (email is sufficient) within [FIVE (5)] business days. POs are subject to inventory availability and THOX's production schedule. THOX may reject a PO that conflicts with this Agreement.

3.3 **Conflicting terms**. The terms of this Agreement govern all transactions between the Parties. Any conflicting or additional terms in Vendor's PO, invoice, or other document are rejected and have no effect.

## 4. Pricing; payment

4.1 **Pricing**. Prices for the Products are set forth in **Schedule A**, exclusive of taxes, shipping, insurance, and other charges. THOX may update pricing on [SIXTY (60) / NINETY (90)] days written notice; updated pricing applies to POs accepted after the effective date of the change.

4.2 **Payment terms**. Vendor shall pay THOX invoices within [NET 30 / NET 45 / 50% UPON PO / 50% PRIOR TO SHIPMENT] days of invoice date. Late payments accrue interest at [ONE AND ONE-HALF PERCENT (1.5%) PER MONTH / THE MAXIMUM RATE PERMITTED BY APPLICABLE LAW], whichever is lower.

4.3 **Method of payment**. Vendor shall pay by [ACH / WIRE TRANSFER / CHECK] to the account designated by THOX in the invoice.

4.4 **Taxes**. Prices exclude all sales, use, value-added, excise, and similar taxes. Vendor is responsible for all such taxes other than taxes on THOX's net income. Vendor shall provide THOX with a valid resale exemption certificate for sales-tax-exempt purchases.

4.5 **Credit hold**. THOX may withhold shipment of Products if Vendor has any past-due invoices or exceeds the credit limit established by THOX.

## 5. Delivery; risk of loss; title

5.1 **Shipping terms**. Products are shipped [FOB Origin / FCA Origin / EXW THOX facility / DDP destination] (the "**Shipping Term**"). Title and risk of loss pass to Vendor upon delivery to the common carrier at THOX's shipping location, unless otherwise specified.

5.2 **Shipping carrier**. THOX selects the carrier unless Vendor specifies an alternative in the PO. Shipping costs are billed to Vendor.

5.3 **Delivery dates**. Delivery dates in accepted POs are estimates. THOX shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet the requested ship date. Late delivery is not a breach of this Agreement unless caused by THOX's gross negligence or willful misconduct.

5.4 **Acceptance and rejection**. Vendor shall inspect Products within [TEN (10)] business days of delivery. Products not rejected in writing within that window are deemed accepted. Rejected Products must conform to the warranty claim procedure in Section 8.

If Vendor fails to meet the Minimum, THOX may, at its option: (a) terminate the [exclusive] appointment and convert Vendor to a non-exclusive arrangement, or (b) invoice Vendor for the shortfall at the agreed unit price.

## 7. THOX intellectual property; brand use

7.1 **No transfer**. This Agreement does not grant Vendor any ownership of, or license to, any THOX intellectual property other than the limited license in Section 7.2.

7.2 **Brand license**. THOX grants Vendor a non-exclusive, non-transferable, royalty-free license during the Term to use THOX's name, logos, and product images solely to market and resell the Products in accordance with the THOX brand guidelines, which THOX shall make available upon request. Vendor shall not modify THOX trademarks or use them in a manner that disparages THOX or its products.

7.3 **No reverse engineering**. Vendor shall not reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble the Products or any THOX firmware or software, except to the extent permitted by applicable law.

7.4 **THOX firmware**. The Products contain THOX firmware and software licensed under the THOX End User License Agreement available at [URL]. Vendor shall not alter the firmware or remove the EULA from the Product or its packaging.

## 8. Limited warranty

8.1 **Warranty**. THOX warrants to Vendor that, for a period of [TWELVE (12) MONTHS] from the date of delivery to Vendor (the "**Warranty Period**"), the Products will conform in all material respects to the Specifications and will be free from defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

8.2 **Exclusions**. The warranty does not cover damage caused by: (a) misuse, abuse, or neglect; (b) modification by anyone other than THOX; (c) unauthorized repair; (d) accident or force majeure; or (e) failure to follow the Specifications and user manual.

8.3 **Remedy**. THOX's sole obligation and Vendor's exclusive remedy for breach of the warranty is, at THOX's option: (a) repair of the defective Product, (b) replacement of the defective Product with a conforming Product, or (c) refund of the price paid for the defective Product. The replacement Product is warranted for the remainder of the original Warranty Period or thirty (30) days from replacement, whichever is longer.

8.4 **Procedure**. Vendor shall submit a written warranty claim within the Warranty Period, including a description of the defect, the Product serial number, and supporting documentation. THOX shall respond within [TEN (10)] business days with disposition instructions.

8.5 **No other warranties**. EXCEPT FOR THE EXPRESS WARRANTY IN THIS SECTION 8, THE PRODUCTS ARE PROVIDED "AS IS" AND THOX DISCLAIMS ALL OTHER WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING WITHOUT LIMITATION THE IMPLIED WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, NON-INFRINGEMENT, AND ANY WARRANTY ARISING FROM COURSE OF DEALING OR USAGE OF TRADE.

## 9. Confidentiality

The Parties' obligations of confidentiality are governed by the Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement separately executed between the Parties, or, if no such NDA is in effect, by the confidentiality provisions of this Section 9. Each Party shall: (a) hold the other Party's Confidential Information in strict confidence, (b) use such Confidential Information solely for purposes of this Agreement, and (c) protect such Confidential Information using at least a reasonable degree of care.

## 10. Indemnification

10.1 **THOX indemnification**. THOX shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Vendor and its officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any third-party claims, damages, losses, and reasonable attorneys' fees arising out of any claim that the Products, as delivered by THOX, infringe a valid US patent, copyright, or trademark of a third party. THOX's obligations under this Section 10.1 are conditioned on Vendor: (a) promptly notifying THOX in writing of the claim, (b) giving THOX sole control over the defense and settlement, and (c) providing reasonable cooperation at THOX's expense.

10.2 **Vendor indemnification**. Vendor shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless THOX and its officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any third-party claims, damages, losses, and reasonable attorneys' fees arising out of: (a) Vendor's breach of this Agreement, (b) Vendor's negligence or willful misconduct, (c) Vendor's marketing or representations about the Products that exceed or contradict THOX's published Specifications, or (d) Vendor's combination of the Products with non-THOX products where the combination causes the alleged infringement or harm.

10.3 **Exclusive remedy**. The indemnification obligations in this Section 10 are the Parties' sole and exclusive remedies for the claims described.

## 11. Term and termination

11.1 **Term**. This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for [ONE (1) / TWO (2)] years, automatically renewing for successive one-year terms unless either Party gives written notice of non-renewal at least [SIXTY (60) / NINETY (90)] days before the end of the then-current term.

11.2 **Termination for convenience**. Either Party may terminate this Agreement for any reason on [SIXTY (60) / NINETY (90)] days written notice to the other Party.

11.3 **Termination for cause**. Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately on written notice if the other Party: (a) materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within thirty (30) days of written notice, (b) becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy, or (c) ceases to do business.

11.4 **Effect of termination**. Upon termination: (a) all accepted POs continue to ship and invoice per their terms unless mutually canceled, (b) Vendor's right to use THOX trademarks terminates and Vendor shall cease all such use within thirty (30) days, (c) each Party returns or destroys the other Party's Confidential Information, and (d) Sections 4 (Payment), 7 (IP), 8 (Warranty), 9 (Confidentiality), 10 (Indemnification), and 14 (General) survive.

## 12. Limitation of liability

EXCEPT FOR (A) BREACH OF CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS, (B) INDEMNIFICATION OBLIGATIONS, (C) WILLFUL MISCONDUCT OR GROSS NEGLIGENCE, OR (D) AMOUNTS PAYABLE TO THIRD PARTIES UNDER A SETTLEMENT OR JUDGMENT, NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE TO THE OTHER FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, SPECIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES, INCLUDING LOST PROFITS OR LOST DATA, EVEN IF ADVISED OF THE POSSIBILITY OF SUCH DAMAGES. THE TOTAL CUMULATIVE LIABILITY OF EITHER PARTY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL AMOUNTS PAID OR PAYABLE BY VENDOR TO THOX UNDER THIS AGREEMENT IN THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS PRECEDING THE EVENT GIVING RISE TO THE CLAIM.

## 13. Insurance

Vendor shall maintain, at its own expense, throughout the Term: (a) commercial general liability insurance with limits of not less than $1,000,000 per occurrence and $2,000,000 aggregate; (b) workers' compensation insurance as required by applicable law; and (c) if Vendor takes physical possession of Products, inland marine or warehouse insurance covering the Products at full replacement cost. Vendor shall provide THOX with certificates of insurance upon request.

## 14. General

14.1 **Independent contractors**. The Parties are independent contractors. Nothing in this Agreement creates any partnership, joint venture, agency, franchise, employment, or fiduciary relationship.

14.2 **No third-party beneficiaries**. This Agreement is for the sole benefit of the Parties and their permitted assigns and is not intended to confer any rights or remedies on any third party.

14.3 **Assignment**. Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the other Party's prior written consent, except to a successor in a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets, upon written notice.

14.4 **Force majeure**. Neither Party is liable for any failure or delay in performance (other than payment obligations) caused by events beyond its reasonable control, including acts of God, natural disasters, war, terrorism, civil unrest, government action, pandemic, labor disputes, supply chain disruption, or carrier failure.

14.5 **Compliance with laws**. Each Party shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations in performing this Agreement, including US export control laws (EAR, ITAR), anti-corruption laws (FCPA), and applicable data privacy laws.

14.6 **Anti-corruption**. Vendor shall not, directly or indirectly, offer or pay any bribe, kickback, or other improper payment in connection with the Products or this Agreement.

14.7 **Notices**. All notices shall be in writing, delivered to the addresses above (or as updated in writing), by email with confirmation of receipt, certified mail return receipt requested, or nationally recognized overnight courier.

14.8 **Governing law and venue**. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of [NEVADA / TEXAS], without regard to conflict-of-laws principles. Any dispute shall be resolved exclusively in the state or federal courts located in [COUNTY], [STATE], and the Parties consent to such jurisdiction and venue.

14.9 **Entire agreement; amendment; waiver**. This Agreement, together with all Schedules, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements. This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by both Parties. No waiver is effective unless in writing.

14.10 **Severability**. If any provision is held unenforceable, the remainder continues in full force; the unenforceable provision is modified minimally to make it enforceable while preserving the Parties' intent.

14.11 **Counterparts; electronic signatures**. Executed in counterparts, each an original; electronic signatures are deemed originals.
