## 6. Limited warranty

Each Product carries a 12-month limited warranty from the date of delivery. The warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal classroom use. The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, modification, accident, or normal wear. Detailed warranty terms are at [URL].

For classroom deployments, THOX recommends ordering a small percentage of spare units (typical: 5-10% above the headcount) to absorb the inevitable wear and damage during the academic year.

## 8. Student privacy

THOX devices operate locally on the Institution's network by default and do not transmit student personal information to THOX unless the Institution explicitly enables a cloud feature. Where the Institution enables any cloud feature, the THOX Privacy Policy at [URL] governs. THOX is committed to FERPA-compatible operation in US educational settings and to GDPR / UK-GDPR compliance for institutions in scope.

THOX is willing to enter into a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with the Institution upon request to formalize the data protection terms for any cloud-enabled deployment.

## 9. Right to publish

THOX will share any such reference with the Institution for review before publication. The Institution may revoke consent at any time, and THOX will discontinue use within a reasonable time after notice.
